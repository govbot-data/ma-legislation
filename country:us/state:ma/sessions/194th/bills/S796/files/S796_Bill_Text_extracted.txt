An Act relative to reducing unnecessary delays in patient care.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (b) of section 25 of chapter 176O of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the words “2 business
3 days” and inserting in place thereof the following:- 24 hours
4 SECTION 2. Said subsection (b) of said section 25 of said chapter 176O is hereby
5 further amended by inserting at the end thereof the following:- Payers and entities acting for
6 payers under contract shall not use appeals processes or other administrative means to delay
7 responding a to completed prior authorization request from a provider in accordance with this
8 subsection. The division shall conduct audits to ensure compliance with this subsection.
