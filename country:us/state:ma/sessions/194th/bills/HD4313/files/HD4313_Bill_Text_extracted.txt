An Act reducing limited liability company formation fees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 12D of Chapter 156C of the General Laws, as so appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the following:- “The fee for the filing of
3 the certificate of organization required by subsection (a) shall be five hundred dollars. The fee
4 for the filing of the annual report required by subsection (c) shall be five hundred dollars.” and
5 inserting in place thereof the following words:- “The fee for the filing of the certificate of
6 organization required by subsection (a) shall be one hundred and fifty dollars. The fee for the
7 filing of the annual report required by subsection (c) shall be one hundred and fifty dollars.”
