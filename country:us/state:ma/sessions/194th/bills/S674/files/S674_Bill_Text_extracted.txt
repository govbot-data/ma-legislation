_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the creation of the Carbon Sequestration Support Fund.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 There shall be established and set up on the books of the commonwealth the Carbon
2 Sequestration Support Fund, the purpose of which shall be to provide grants to support actions to
3 increase the sequestration of carbon from the atmosphere.
