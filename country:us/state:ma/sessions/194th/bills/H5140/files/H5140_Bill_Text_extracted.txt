An Act creating parity among Massachusetts beaches.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the Massachusetts Division of
2 Fisheries and Wildlife shall not prohibit or exclude any beach management program from
3 utilizing all legally authorized shorebird nesting mitigation tools available under the
4 Massachusetts Habitat Conservation Plan.
