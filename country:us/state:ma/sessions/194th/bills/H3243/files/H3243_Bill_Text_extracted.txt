_______________
An Act relative to disabled service-connected veterans and the motor vehicle excise tax.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of chapter 60A, as so appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting after the eighth paragraph the following paragraph:
3 "The excise imposed by this section shall not apply to a motor vehicle owned and
4 registered by a veteran, as defined in section 7 of chapter 4, who according to the records of the
5 United States Veterans Administration is one hundred percent service connected disabled as
6 defined by title 38 section 101 of the Code."
