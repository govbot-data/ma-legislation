An Act relative to sex offenders.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Paragraph (b) of subsection (2) of section 178K of chapter 6 of the General Laws, as so
2 amended by chapter 38 of the Acts of 2013, is hereby amended by inserting, in the second
3 sentence, after the word “Investigation.” the following sentences:-
4 No sex offender designated as a level 2 or 3 offenders shall establish a home address or
5 intended home address or any other living accommodation within 500 feet of the property on
6 which any public or private school, licensed day care center, or any other child care facility is
7 located. Nor shall the offender accept employment within 500 feet of the property on which any
8 public or private school, licensed day care center, or any other child care facility is located.
