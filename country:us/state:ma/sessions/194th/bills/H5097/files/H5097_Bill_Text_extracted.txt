An Act authorizing the city of Salem to convert seasonal licenses for the sale of alcoholic
beverages to be drunk on the premises to annual licenses for the sale of alcoholic beverages to be
drunk on the premises.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. (a) Notwithstanding sections 17 and 17A of chapter 138 of the General
2 Laws, the licensing authority of the city of Salem may convert 1 seasonal license for the sale of
3 all alcoholic beverages to be drunk on the premises under section 12 of said chapter 138,
4 currently issued to Amazing Pizza LLC d/b/a Amazing Pizza and used at 62 Wharf Street to an
5 annual license for the sale of wine and malt beverages and cordials to be drunk on the premises
6 under said section 12 of said chapter 138 to Amazing Pizza LLC d/b/a Amazing Pizza and used
7 at 62 Wharf Street. The license converted under this section shall be subject to all of said chapter
8 138 except sections 17 and 17A.
9 (b) The licensing authority shall not approve the transfer of the license converted
10 pursuant to this section to any other location but it may grant the license to a new applicant at the
11 same location if the applicant files with the licensing authority a letter from the department of
12 revenue and a letter from the department of unemployment assistance indicating that the license
1 of 3

13 is in good standing with those departments and that all applicable taxes, fees, and contributions
14 have been paid.
15 (c) If a licensee terminates or fails to renew a license converted under this section or any
16 such license is cancelled, revoked or no longer in use, it shall be returned physically, with all of
17 the legal rights, privileges and restrictions pertaining thereto, to the licensing authority which
18 may, within 3 years after such return, then grant the license to a new applicant at the same
19 location and under the same conditions as specified in this section, otherwise such license shall
20 dissolve.
21 SECTION 2. (a) Notwithstanding sections 17 and 17A of chapter 138 of the General
22 Laws, the licensing authority of the city of Salem may convert 1 seasonal license for the sale of
23 all alcoholic beverages to be drunk on the premises under section 12 of said chapter 138,
24 currently issued to Maitland Farm LLC d/b/a Maitland Farm Kitchen and Spirits and used at 84
25 Derby Street to an annual license for the sale of all alcoholic beverages to be drunk on the
26 premises under said section 12 of said chapter 138 to Maitland Farm LLC d/b/a Maitland Farm
27 Kitchen and Spirits and for use at 84 Derby Street. The license converted under this section shall
28 be subject to all of said chapter 138 except sections 17 and 17A.
29 (b) The licensing authority shall not approve the transfer of the license converted under
30 this section to any other location, but it may grant the license to a new applicant at the same
31 location if the applicant files with the licensing authority a letter from the department of revenue
32 and a letter from the department of unemployment assistance indicating that the license is in
33 good standing with those departments and that all applicable taxes, fees and contributions have
34 been paid.
2 of 3

35 (c) If a licensee terminates or fails to renew a license converted under this section or any
36 such license is cancelled, revoked or no longer in use, it shall be returned physically, with all of
37 the legal rights, privileges and restrictions pertaining thereto, to the licensing authority which
38 may, within 3 years after such return, then grant the license to a new applicant at the same
39 location and under the same conditions as specified in this section, otherwise such license shall
40 dissolve.
41 SECTION 3. (a) The city of Salem may charge a fee for the conversion of the licenses
42 authorized in sections 1 and 2 which shall be paid by the licensee at the time of issuance unless
43 the licensing authority agrees to enter into a contract with the licensees to allow the division of
44 the fee into multiple payments over time from the licensees. If the city elects to accept multiple
45 payments over time from the licensees, the option shall be made available, upon request, to all
46 qualified applicants for a license.
47 (b) Any fee collected by the city of Salem for the licenses converted pursuant to sections
48 1 and 2, which are greater than the amount of the fees charged for an annual renewal of a similar
49 license issued by the city shall be deposited into the city's economic development account and
50 expended by it in a manner consistent with the purposes of such account.
51 SECTION 4. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
