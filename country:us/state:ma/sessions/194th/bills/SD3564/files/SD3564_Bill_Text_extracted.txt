SENATE DOCKET, NO. 3564 FILED ON: 1/8/2026
Senate, January 8, 2026 -- Order (Senator Rodrigues) relative to procedures to be followed when
considering the Senate Bill to prevent property tax bill shocks (Senate, No. 1933)
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
1 Ordered, That notwithstanding Senate Rule 7 or any other rule to the contrary, the Senate
2 Bill to prevent property tax bill shocks (Senate, No. 1933) to be reported by the committee on
3 Ways and Means with a recommended new draft (Senate, No. 2899) on Thursday, January 8,
4 2026, and read into the Senate Journal on Thursday, January 8, 2026, shall be placed in the
5 Orders of the Day for a second reading for Thursday, January 15, 2026, for immediate
6 consideration with the Ways and Means new draft pending.
7 All amendments shall be filed electronically in the office of the Clerk of the Senate by
8 3:30 P.M, on Monday, January 12, 2026. All such amendments shall be second reading-
9 amendments to the proposed Senate Ways and Means new draft (Senate, No. 2899), but further
10 amendments in the third degree to such amendments shall be in order. The Clerk shall further
11 specify the procedure and format for filing all amendments, consistent with this order.

12 After the bill as amended is ordered to a third reading, it shall immediately be read a third
13 time and the question shall then immediately be on passing it to be engrossed, and no
14 amendments shall be in order at the third reading of the bill unless recommended by the
15 committee on Bills in the Third Reading.
