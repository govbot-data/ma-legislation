An Act relative to the financing of motor vehicle purchases.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 4 of chapter 93B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in line 281, the word " or".
3 SECTION 2. Said section 4 of chapter 93B, as so appearing, is hereby further amended
4 by striking out, in line 285, the word "withheld." and inserting in place thereof the following
5 words:- withheld; or.
6 SECTION 3. Subsection d of said section 4 of chapter 93B, as so appearing, is hereby
7 amended by adding the following clause:-
8 (4) to prohibit a purchaser of a motor vehicle from securing financing for the purchase of
9 a motor vehicle from a source other than the motor vehicle dealer.
