An Act improving access to community behavioral health centers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 32A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 section 34 the following new section:-
3 Section 35. (a) For the purposes of this section, the following words shall have the
4 following meanings:
5 “Behavioral health bundled services”, services delivered by a community behavioral
6 health center as specified in 101 CMR 305.00 for the evaluation, diagnosis, treatment, care
7 coordination, management, or peer support of patients with mental health, developmental or
8 substance use disorder, provided that services are reimbursed through a flat rate per encounter.
9 “Community Behavioral Health Centers”, a clinic licensed by the department of public
10 health and regulated pursuant to 130 CMR 448.00.
11 (b) Any health plan offered by the commission shall provide to an active or retired
12 employee of the commonwealth who is insured under the group insurance commission benefits

13 on a nondiscriminatory basis for medically necessary behavioral health bundled services
14 delivered through community behavioral health centers.
