An Act relative to witness intimidation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (b) of section 13B of chapter 268 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following sentence:- If the misconduct
3 was committed while the person was armed, such person shall be punished by imprisonment in
4 the state prison for not more than 20 years or by imprisonment in the house of correction for not
5 more than 2½ years or by a fine of not more than $10,000 or by both such fine and
6 imprisonment.
