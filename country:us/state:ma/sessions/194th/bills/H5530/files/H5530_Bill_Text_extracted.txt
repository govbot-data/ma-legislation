HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, June 24, 2026.
The committee on Public Safety and Homeland Security, to whom was
referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4697) of Jeffrey
Rosario Turco relative to the training, certification, and recognition of
constables and civil deputy sheriffs, reports (under the provisions of
House Rule 27) recommending that the accompanying order (House, No.
5530) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
DANIEL CAHILL.
