An Act enhancing the issuance of citations for cruel conditions for animals.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 35WW of chapter 10 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the figure “62”, in line 17, the following
3 words:- , fines collected pursuant to section 37 of chapter 129
4 SECTION 2. Section 37 of chapter 129 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
5 amended by inserting after the fourth sentence the following sentence:- A fine assessed under
6 this section shall be deposited into the Homeless Animal Prevention and Care Fund established

8 SECTION 3. Section 174E of chapter 140 of the General Laws, as so appearing, is
9 hereby amended by striking subsection (g) and inserting in place thereof the following
10 subsection:-
11 (g) No person owning or keeping a domestic animal shall subject the animal to “cruel
12 conditions.”
13 For the purposes of this section, a “domestic animal” is all animals, regardless of their
14 purpose or use, including livestock, that are kept as a domestic animal.
15 For the purposes of this subsection, "cruel conditions” includes, but is not limited to, the
16 following:
17 (1) exposure to excessive animal waste, garbage, non-potable water, excessive noxious
18 odors that create a health threat to people or animals, dangerous objects or other animals that
19 could injure or kill an animal upon contact, other circumstances that could cause harm to the
20 health or safety of the animal based on species, age or physical condition; or failure to provide
21 access to appropriate food and water based on the animal’s species, age and physical condition.
22 (2) lack of protection when wind or environmental or weather conditions pose an adverse
23 risk to the health or safety of the animal based on the animal’s species, age, or physical
24 condition.
25 SECTION 4. Subsection (h) of section 174E of said chapter 140 is hereby amended by
26 inserting at the beginning thereof the following:-
27 A person who violates this section shall: (i) for a first offense, be issued a written
28 warning or punished by a fine of not more than $50; (ii) for a second offense, be punished by a
2 of 3

29 fine of not more than $200; and (iii) for a third or subsequent offense, be punished by a fine of
30 not more than $500; provided, however, that for a third or subsequent offense, the animal may be
31 subject to impoundment in a local shelter or appropriate facility at the owner's, keeper’s or
32 guardian's expense pending compliance with this section, or loss of ownership of the animal.
