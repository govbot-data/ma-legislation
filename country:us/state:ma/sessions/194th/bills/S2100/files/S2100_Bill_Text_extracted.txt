SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2100
By Mr. Keenan, a petition (accompanied by resolve, Senate, No. 2100) of John F. Keenan and
Rebecca L. Rausch that provisions be made for an investigation and study by a special
commission on additional regular compensation for certain members and annual expenses for
members of general court. Rules of the two branches, acting concurrently.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
Resolve creating a special commission on additional regular compensation for certain members
and annual expenses for members of general court.
1 Resolved, that there shall be a special commission established to review the amounts for
2 certain members pursuant to section 9B of chapter 3 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
3 2022 Official Edition, and the annual expenses for members of general court pursuant to section
4 9C of said chapter 3, as so appearing. The commission shall report its findings, including any
5 proposed legislation, to the house and senate committees on ways and means and the joint
6 committee on public service no later than December 31, 2026.
7 The 9 members of the commission shall consist of 1 who shall be appointed by the
8 speaker of the house, 1 who shall be appointed by the minority leader of the house, 1 who shall
9 be appointed by the president of the senate, 1 who shall be appointed by the minority leader of
10 the senate, 1 who shall be a representative of the Center for State Policy Analysis, 1 who shall be
11 a representative of the League of Women Voters of Massachusetts, 1 who shall be a
12 representative of the Massachusetts Taxpayers Foundation, 1 who shall be a representative of
13 MASSPIRG and 1 who shall be a representative of the Pioneer Institute.
