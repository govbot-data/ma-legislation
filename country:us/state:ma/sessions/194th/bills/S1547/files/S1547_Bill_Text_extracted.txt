An Act relative to the board of registration in naturopathy.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Clause (ii) of subsection (b) of section 267 of chapter 112 of the General
2 Laws is hereby amended by striking out the words “controlled substance or prescription drug
3 under chapter 94C” and by inserting in place thereof the words:- Schedule I-V controlled
4 substance.
5 SECTION 2. Clause (iv) of subsection (a) of section 268 of said chapter 112 is hereby
6 amended by inserting, after the word “equivalent”, the following words:- state exam that existed
7 prior to 1987.
8 SECTION 3. Subsection (c) of section 269 of said chapter 112, as so inserted, is hereby
9 amended by inserting, after the word “board”, the following words:- or a state exam that existed
10 prior to 1987.
