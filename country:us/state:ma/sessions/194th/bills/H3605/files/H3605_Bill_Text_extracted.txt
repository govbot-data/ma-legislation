In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to vanity plate fees.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1 Notwithstanding any special or general law, rule or regulation to the
2 contrary, the registry of motor vehicles shall only charge a single special fee for a vanity plate
3 registration. Any and all subsequent registration renewal fees shall be at the same amount as for
4 standard non-vanity plates in the commonwealth.
5 SECTION 2 This act shall take effect on January 1st of the year following passage into
6 law.
