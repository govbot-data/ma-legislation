HOUSE . . . . . . . . No. 5574
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, July 13, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3086) of Michelle M. DuBois relative to
providing tax credits to certain employers that provide on-site child-care
for employees; the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3110) of
Carlos González and others relative to offshore taxes; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3194) of Samantha Montaño and others
for legislation to increase the deeds excise to fund emergency assistance
needs of certain low income tenants and homeowners; and the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3261) of Erika Uyterhoeven relative to
establishing a tax on excessive executive compensation, reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5574) ought to
be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
