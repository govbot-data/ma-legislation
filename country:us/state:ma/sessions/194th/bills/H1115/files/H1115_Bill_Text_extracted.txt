HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1965 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1115
By Representatives Consalvo of Boston and Lawn of Watertown, a petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 1115) of Rob Consalvo and Lindsay N. Sabadosa for legislation to provide
health insurance coverage for individuals with vitiligo. Financial Services.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act requiring full health insurance coverage for individuals with vitiligo.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 32A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 section 17Z the following section:-
3 Section 17AA. The commission shall provide, to an active or retired employee of the
4 commonwealth who is insured under the group insurance commission, coverage for treatment of
5 vitiligo as a chronic autoimmune disease including, but not limited to, mental health treatment in
6 connection with vitiligo.
7 SECTION 2. Chapter 118E of the General laws is hereby amended by inserting after
8 section 10Z the following section:-
9 Section 10AA. The division and its contracted health insurers, health plans, health
10 maintenance organizations, behavioral health management firms and third-party administrators
11 under contract to a Medicaid managed care organization or primary care clinician plan shall
1 of 3

12 provide coverage for treatment of vitiligo as a chronic autoimmune disease including, but not
13 limited to, mental health treatment in connection with vitiligo.
14 SECTION 3. Chapter 175 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
15 section 47CCC the following section:-
16 Section 47CCC. Any policy, contract, agreement, plan or certificate of insurance issued,
17 delivered or renewed within or without the commonwealth shall provide coverage for treatment
18 of vitiligo as a chronic autoimmune disease including, but not limited to, mental health treatment
19 in connection with vitiligo.
20 SECTION 4. Chapter 176A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
21 section 8CCC the following section:-
22 Section 8DDD. A contract between a subscriber and the corporation under an individual
23 or group hospital service plan that is delivered, issued or renewed within the commonwealth
24 shall provide coverage for treatment of vitiligo as a chronic autoimmune disease including, but
25 not limited to, mental health treatment in connection with vitiligo.
26 SECTION 5. Chapter 176B of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
27 section 4CCC the following section:-
28 Section 4DDD. Any subscription certificate under an individual or group medical service
29 agreement delivered, issued or renewed within the commonwealth shall provide coverage for
30 treatment of vitiligo as a chronic autoimmune disease including, but not limited to, mental health
31 treatment in connection with vitiligo.

32 SECTION 6. Chapter 176G of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
33 section 4VV the following section:-
34 Section 4WW. Any individual or group health maintenance contract that is issued or
35 renewed within or without the commonwealth shall provide coverage for treatment of vitiligo as
36 a chronic autoimmune disease including, but not limited to, mental health treatment in
37 connection with vitiligo.
