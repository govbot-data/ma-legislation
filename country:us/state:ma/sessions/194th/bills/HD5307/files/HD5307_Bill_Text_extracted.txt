of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The first paragraph of section 8 of chapter 53 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the fourth sentence

5 to the state secretary the names of 2 qualified individuals. One of the individuals shall be

17 Section 8A. If, after the nomination of a candidate for president or vice president by a
18 political party and before the meeting of presidential electors described in section 148 of chapter
19 54, the candidate dies or withdraws as a candidate for that office in accordance with the rules of
20 the political party, the pledge executed by nominees and alternate nominees for presidential
21 elector under section 8 or by substitute presidential electors under section subsection (b) of

23 the political party in accordance with the party’s rules.

26 In submitting the commonwealth’s certificate of ascertainment as required by 3 U.S.C.

35 Section 148A. (a) If an elector is not present to vote at the required time, the elector
36 position shall be considered vacant. The presiding officer of the electors shall appoint an
37 individual as a substitute elector to fill a vacancy as follows:
38 (I) if the alternate elector nominee is present to vote, by appointing the alternate elector
39 nominee for the vacant position.
40 (ii) if the alternate elector nominee for the vacant position is not present to vote, by
41 appointing an elector chosen by lot from among the alternate elector nominees present to vote
42 who were nominated by the same political party or group;
43 (iii) if the number of alternate elector nominees present to vote is insufficient to fill a
44 vacant position pursuant to clauses (i) and (ii), by appointing any immediately available
45 individual who is qualified to serve as an elector and chosen through nomination by and plurality
46 vote of the remaining electors, including nomination and vote by a single elector if only 1 elector
47 remains;
48 (iv) if there is a tie between at least 2 nominees for substitute elector in a vote conducted
49 under clause (iii), by appointing an elector chosen by lot from among those nominees.
50 (b) If all elector positions are vacant and cannot be filled pursuant to subsection (a), the
51 state secretary shall appoint a single presidential elector, with remaining vacant positions to be
52 filled pursuant to clause (iii) or, if necessary, clause (iv) of said subsection (a).
53 (c) To qualify as a substitute elector under subsection (a), an individual who has not
