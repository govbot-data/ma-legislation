An Act relative to the purchase of zero-emission vehicles in green communities.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 10 of Chapter 25A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended, in subsection (c), by striking out the words “(5) purchase only fuel-
3 efficient vehicles for municipal use whenever such vehicles are commercially available and
4 practicable” and inserting in place thereof the following:-
5 “(5) purchase only zero-emission vehicles, as defined in section 16 of said chapter 25A,
6 for municipal use whenever such vehicles are commercially available and practicable; provided,
7 that when such zero-emission vehicles are not commercially available or not practicable, a
8 municipality or other local governmental body shall purchase only fuel-efficient vehicles for
9 municipal use whenever such vehicles are commercially available and practicable”.
