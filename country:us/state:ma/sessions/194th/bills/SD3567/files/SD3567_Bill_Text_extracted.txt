1 Ordered, That notwithstanding Senate Rule 7 or any other rule to the contrary, the Senate
2 Bill authorizing the establishment of a mean tested senior citizen property tax exemption (Senate,

4 (Senate, No. 2902) on Thursday, January 8, 2026, and read into the Senate Journal on Thursday,
