Andres X. Vargas relative to providing for de-escalation tactics and techniques approved by the

_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to establish de-escalation training in student transportation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8A of chapter 90, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition of the
2 General Laws, is hereby amended by inserting after the second paragraph the following
3 paragraph:- There shall be training relative to de-escalation tactics and techniques approved by
4 the commissioner of education required for all school bus operators. If employed, the operator’s
5 employer shall pay for the cost of the de-escalation training.
