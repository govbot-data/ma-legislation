of the same, as follows:

28 The motor vehicle storage facility shall allow the owner of a vehicle, of which the motor
29 vehicle storage facility is lawfully in possession, to remove any possessions in that vehicle that

32 The motor vehicle storage facility shall post in a conspicuous place on the premises and
33 visible from the street, the days and hours that a motor vehicle owner may retrieve a vehicle from

35 department. Any motor vehicle storage facility that maintains a website shall post on its website
36 such days and hours that a motor vehicle owner may retrieve their vehicle from a storage yard.
37 The department may promulgate regulations requiring motor vehicle storage facilities to post in a
38 conspicuous place a notice to customers of other requirements in this chapter.
39 Any person engaged in the towing away, transportation, and storage of motor vehicles
40 shall accept, within the tow vehicle, being operated, and on the premises of a motor vehicle
41 storage facility, the following methods of payment for vehicle removal and storage charges:
42 credit or debit card payments, or cash payment. Such person shall accept payments by any
43 mobile application or digital platform that facilitates electronic money transfers if they ordinarily
44 accept such method of payment within the tow vehicle being operated or on the premises or the
