An Act to clarify the organization of the Lexington Housing Assistance Board.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1: Chapter 192 of the Acts of 2024 shall be amended by: a. replacing Section
2 1 thereof with the following: "There is hereby established an independent, nonprofit housing
3 corporation to be known as the Lexington Housing Assistance Board, Inc. or "LexHAB" which
4 shall be organized and operated exclusively for charitable purposes described in section
5 501(c)(3) of the United States Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended." b. replacing Section
6 11 thereof with the following: "In the event that the board shall be dissolved in accordance with
7 law at any time, all property and interests therein, assets, and rights of said board existing at such
8 time shall be transferred to the town of Lexington, or to a qualified non-profit housing
9 development corporation described in section 501(c)(3) of the United States Internal Revenue
10 Code of 1986, as amended, and designated by the town of Lexington, which property and assets
11 shall be used exclusively for charitable purposes described in section 501(c)(3) of said Internal
12 Revenue Code; and title to all such property and all such rights shall vest in the town of
13 Lexington automatically without the need for further action or instrument, and the town of
1 of 2

14 Lexington shall, to the maximum extent permitted by law, and acting by and through its Select
15 Board, assume, hold and exercise the powers and duties of said board set forth herein with
16 respect to such property and rights so transferred to said town."
