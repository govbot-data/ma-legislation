Per Capita Cost Report
Fiscal Year 2024
Executive Office of Public Safety and Security
March 2025
Maura T. Healey, Governor
Terrence M. Reidy, Secretary of Public Safety and Security

Massachusetts Department of Correction
Per Capita Cost Report
Pursuant to the Massachusetts Department of Correction (DOC) line item 8900-0001 of
the Fiscal Year 2024 General Appropriations Act which states:
“that not later than September 29, 2023, the department shall also report,
in collaboration with the Massachusetts Sheriffs Association, Inc. on fiscal
year 2022 and fiscal year 2023 total costs per inmate by facility and
security level;”
This report provides the estimated average annual costs associated with each incarcerated
person housed within DOC facilities for the fiscal years 2022 and 2023.
