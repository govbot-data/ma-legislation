HOUSE . . . . . . . . No. 5166
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 2, 2026.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom were referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3345) of
Kate Hogan for legislation to designate the blanding turtle as the official
turtle or turtle emblem of the Commonwealth; the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 3373) of Michael P. Kushmerek for legislation to designate
the Spring Peeper as the official frog of the Commonwealth; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3383) of David Paul Linsky for
legislation to designate the song “Roadrunner” as the official rock song of
the Commonwealth; the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3396) of
James M. Murphy for legislation to designate Bell's Seasoning as the
official seasoning of the Commonwealth; and the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 3404) of Edward R. Philips and Steven D'Addieco for
legislation to designate the Eastern Brook Trout as the official freshwater
fish of the Commonwealth, reports recommending that the accompanying
order (House, No. 5166) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.

HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5166
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, March 02, 2026.
1 Ordered, That the committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight be
