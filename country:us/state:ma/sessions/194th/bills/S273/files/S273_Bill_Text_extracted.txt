An Act to reduce lottery littering.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The Massachusetts State Treasurers Office shall instruct the Massachusetts
2 State Lottery to develop a second chance game involving all lottery tickets and scratch tickets
3 that do not contain winning numbers. The purpose of the game shall be to increase the amount of
4 scratch tickets and lottery tickets recycled and reduce the amount of scratch tickets and lottery
5 tickets thrown away or littered.
6 SECTION 2. The second chance game must be operational no later than 180 days of the
7 passage of this bill.
