154 SECTION 27. The Massachusetts Department of Transportation shall, within one year of
155 the effective date of this Act, update the regulations contained in 700 C.M.R.7.00 et seq to
156 permit any individual owing tolls, fines, fees, or penalties under the EZDriveMA program to
157 seek a waiver, reduction or payment plan consistent with the procedure set forth in section 3 of
158 chapter 90C of the General Laws. These regulations shall provide that: (i) payments under a
159 payment plan shall not exceed 2% of an individual’s non-exempt monthly net income; (ii) upon
160 receipt of the first payment due under a monthly payment plan, the department shall not charge
161 any additional fines or fees on existing debts for late payment or failure to pay; (iii) the
162 department shall accept automatic monthly payments by credit or debit card on file with the
163 department and payments made by check or money order received by mail or in person; (iv)
164 payment plans may be modified upon a determination that there has been a material and
165 substantial change in circumstances; (v) any time an individual fails to make a timely payment,

169 the department may take appropriate action to pursue the outstanding debt through permissible
170 enforcement efforts; and (vii) license suspension, license nonrenewal, and registration
171 suspension shall not be imposed as a penalty for nonpayment.
