An Act designating a portion of state highway Route 7A in the town of Lenox as the Ross B.
Dindio Memorial highway.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The portion of state highway route 7A in the town of Lenox from mile marker 2 to mile
2 marker 2.62 shall be designated and known as the Ross B. Dindio Memorial highway. The
3 Massachusetts Department of Transportation is hereby directed to erect and maintain suitable
4 markers along said highway bearing said designation in accordance with the standards of the
5 department.
