An Act relative to legal advertisements in online-only newspapers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (b) of section 13 of chapter 4 of the General Laws, as appearing
2 in the 2020 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following sentence:- Provided
3 further, that for an online-only newspaper that does not maintain a print publication, publication
4 on its own website and on a statewide website shall satisfy the publication requirement.
5 SECTION 2. Subsection (d) of said section 13 of said chapter 4, as so appearing, is
6 hereby amended by adding the following sentence:- For an online-only newspaper, an error in a
7 legal notice published on the online-only newspaper's website or the statewide website that is the
8 result of (1) an error of the website operator; or (2) a temporary website outage or service
9 interruption that prevents the publication or display of a legal notice on the website, shall not
10 constitute a defect in publication of the legal notice; provided, however, that the legal notice
1 of 2

11 appears correctly on either the online-only newspaper's website or the statewide website, and
12 satisfies all other legal notice requirements.
