An Act relative to timely retirement payment.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Paragraph (B) of subdivision (1) of Section 13 of Chapter 32 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2020 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following after the
3 words “allowance becomes effective.”, the following:
4 “In the event circumstances beyond the retirement board’s control prevent the calculation
5 of the full monthly payment by the first such full payment date, the retirement board shall pay
6 90% of its estimated full monthly payment until such time as the circumstances beyond the
7 retirement board’s control are corrected. On an annual basis, the retirement board will notify the
8 House and the Senate of all circumstances beyond the retirement board’s control that prevented
9 the calculation of the full monthly payment by the first such full payment date.”
