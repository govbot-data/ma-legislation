HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5398
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, May 11, 2026.
1 Ordered, That the committee on Public Service be authorized to sit during a recess of the
2 General Court to make an investigation and study of House document numbered 4443, relative to
3 the maximum age requirement for original appointment to the position of firefighter or police
4 officer in the city of Haverhill.
5 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
6 and its recommendations, if any, together with drafts of legislation necessary to carry such
7 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
8 on or before December 31, 2026.
