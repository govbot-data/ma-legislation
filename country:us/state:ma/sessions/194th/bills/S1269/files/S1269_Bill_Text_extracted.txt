An Act relative to the tracking of medical malpractice cases.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special to the contrary the administrator of the trial court
2 shall develop and implement a system to track and categorize any civil action malpractice, error
3 or mistake against a provider of health care. Annually by December 31 the administrator of the
4 trial court shall submit the number of any civil action malpractice, error or mistake against a
5 provider of health care to the clerks of the house and senate and the joint committee on the
6 judiciary.
