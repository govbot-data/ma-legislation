Pursuant to M.G.L. c. 29, § 2TT, the Office of the Comptroller hereby submits this report of the
activities of the Liability Management and Reduction Fund (LMRF) to the House and Senate
Committees on Ways and Means, the Secretary of Administration and Finance, and the Attorney
General.
Attachment 1 shows the Fund’s financial statement for Fiscal Year 2025, as published in the
Commonwealth Statutory Basis Financial Report (SBFR) on page 154.
Attachment 2 shows the detail by department of the premiums charged. These are derived from
each department’s five-year average of tort payments and litigation costs from Fiscal Year 2020
through Fiscal Year 2024.
In summary:
• As Attachment 1 shows, total LMRF revenues for Fiscal Year 2025 were $1.88 million,
and expenditures were $2.142 million. Because revenues were less than expenditures by
$0.262 million, the fund balance decreased from $3.459 million at the beginning of the
fiscal year to $3.197 million at the end.
• The total of premiums by department shown in Attachment 2 is $1.8 million. This
constitutes the majority of the $1.88 million revenue figure discussed above, with the
balance coming from interest and administrative fees.
• Note that this letter quotes figures in millions. The exhibit from the SBFR shows amounts
in thousands; the department detail exhibit is expressed in dollars and cents.
