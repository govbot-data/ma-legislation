HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, January 12, 2026.
The committee on Children, Families and Persons with Disabilities, to
whom was referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 250) of
Bradley H. Jones, Jr., and others relative to foster care recruitment, reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 4905) ought to be
adopted.
For the committee,
JAY D. LIVINGSTONE.
