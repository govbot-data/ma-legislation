HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 2, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3078) of David F. DeCoste for legislation
to provide municipalities with the option to freeze residential tax rate or
valuation for the elderly with means tested criteria; and the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3967) of Christopher Richard Flanagan
for legislation to further regulate surcharges on real property, reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5163) ought to be
adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
