An Act relative to equitable towing rates.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 6B of Chapter 159B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022

5 amended by inserting, in line 14, after the word “persons”, the following words:- or any
6 commercially registered vehicle not requiring a CDL license.
