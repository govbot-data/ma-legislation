In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act exempting precious metal purchases from state sales tax.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Clause (ll) of section 6 of chapter 64H of the General Laws, as appearing in
2 the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the clause and inserting in place
3 thereof the following:-
4 "(ll) Sales of (i) rare coins of numismatic value; (ii) gold or silver bullion or coins; or (iii)
5 gold or silver tender of any nation traded and sold according to its value as precious metal. The
6 word ''bullion'' shall not include fabricated precious metal which has been processed or
7 manufactured for industrial, professional or artistic uses.”
