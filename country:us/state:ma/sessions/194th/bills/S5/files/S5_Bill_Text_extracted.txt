SENATE DOCKET, NO. 16 FILED ON: 1/3/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5
Senate, January 6, 2025 -- Order (Senator Spilka) establishing a temporary committee on rules
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
Senate, January 06, 2025.
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
1 Ordered, That, notwithstanding Senate Rule 12 or any other rule to the contrary, until
2 such time as a permanent committee is appointed, there shall be a temporary committee on rules
3 to consist of 9 members, 2 of whom shall be appointed by the minority leader; and
4 Be it further ordered that, there shall be a subcommittee of the temporary committee on rules
5 chaired by the vice chair and to consist of members of the temporary committee appointed by the
6 chair. The subcommittee shall receive and respond as appropriate to any matters referred to it by
7 the Senate in relation to chapter 250 of the acts of 2024.
