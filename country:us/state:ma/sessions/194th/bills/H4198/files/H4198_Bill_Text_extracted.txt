An Act to promote fairness between traditional public schools and regional vocational schools.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding the provisions of section 6 of Chapter 70 of the General laws, no
2 regional vocational school district shall receive an annual contribution from a member
3 municipality greater than the percentage increase appropriated for the member’s traditional
4 public school district’s annual budget percentage increase.
