HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 9, 2025.
The committee on Transportation, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3803) of David T. Vieira relative to the
suspension or revocation of motor vehicle licenses for the failure to pay
municipal fines; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3807)
of Tommy Vitolo relative to out of state operator motor vehicle license
applications, reports recommending that the accompanying order (House,
No. 4787) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JAMES ARCIERO.
