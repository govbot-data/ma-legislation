An Act relative to extending certain COVID-19 measures adopted during the state of emergency.
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
extend certain COVID-19 measures adopted during the state of emergency, therefore it is hereby
declared to be an emergency law, necessary for the immediate preservation of the public health.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 30A of chapter 20 of the acts of 2021, as most recently amended by
2 section 40 of chapter 2 of the acts of 2023, is hereby further amended by striking out the words
3 “March 31, 2025” and inserting in place thereof the following words:- June 30, 2027.
4 SECTION 2. Section 44B of chapter 22 of the acts of 2022, inserted by section 50 of said
5 chapter 2, is hereby amended by striking out the words “March 31, 2025” and inserting in place
6 thereof the following words:- June 30, 2027.
