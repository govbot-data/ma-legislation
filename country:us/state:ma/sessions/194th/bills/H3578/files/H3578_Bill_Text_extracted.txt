An Act expanding the definition of beverage.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 321 of chapter 94 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out the definition of “Beverage” and inserting in
3 place thereof the following definition:-
4 ''Beverage'', soda water or similar carbonated soft drinks, mineral water, water and
5 alcoholic beverages, as defined in section 1 of chapter 138, but shall not include dairy products
6 or natural fruit juices.
7 SECTION 2. Said section 321 of said chapter 94 is hereby further amended by inserting
8 in line 23, after the word “commonwealth”, the following words:- , but shall not include a
9 brewery, distillery, cidery or winery that only sells its own beverages on its premises.
