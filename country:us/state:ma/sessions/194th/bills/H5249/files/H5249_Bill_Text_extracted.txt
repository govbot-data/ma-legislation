HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 19, 2026.
The committee on Agriculture and Fisheries, to whom were referred
the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 136) of Steven Owens and
Kristin E. Kassner for legislation to further regulate the use of sludge from
a municipal, commercial or industrial wastewater treatment plant as an
agricultural product; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
141) of Marcus S. Vaughn for legislation to prohibit foreign governments
from holding any interest in agricultural land, reports (under the
provisions of House Rule 27) recommending that the accompanying order
(House, No. 5249) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
AARON L. SAUNDERS.
