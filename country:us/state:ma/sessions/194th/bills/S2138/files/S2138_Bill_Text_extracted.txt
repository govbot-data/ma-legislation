An Act reducing the costs for small businesses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 12 of chapter 156C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out subsection (d) and inserting in place thereof
3 the following:-
4 (d) The fee for filing of the certificate of organization required by subsection (a) shall be
5 two hundred and fifty dollars. The fee for the filing of the annual report required by subsection
6 (c) shall be two hundred and fifty dollars. Such fees shall be paid to the state secretary at the time
7 the certificate of organization or the annual report is filed.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall be effective beginning January 1, 2026.
