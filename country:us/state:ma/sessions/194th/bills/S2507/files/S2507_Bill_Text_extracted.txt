An Act establishing the Gloucester Fisheries Commission.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 260 of the Acts of 1956 is hereby amended by striking out section
2 1 as most recently amended by section 1 of Chapter 338 of the Acts of 1984, and inserting in
3 place thereof the following:-- " Section 1. There is hereby created a body politic and corporate to
4 be known as the Gloucester Fisheries Commission which shall be deemed to be a public
5 instrumentality for the purposes of this act and shall have an official seal as determined by the
6 membership of the commission."
7 SECTION 2. Said chapter 260 is hereby further amended by striking out section 2 and
8 inserting in place thereof the following section:-- "Section 2. Said commission shall be set up
9 and sponsored by the City of Gloucester to investigate, advocate and recommend measures for
10 the promotion, preservation and protection of the Gloucester Fishing Industry."
1 of 2

11 SECTION 3. Chapter 260 of the Acts of 1956 is hereby amended by striking Section 3 as
12 most recently amended by Section 3 of Chapter 338 of the Acts of 1984, and inserting in place
13 thereof the following section:-- " Section 3. The commission shall consist of seven members, one
14 of whom shall be the mayor or their designee. Six members shall be appointed by the mayor and
15 confirmed by the city council for a three-year term. The mayor may appoint, with confirmation
16 by the city council, two alternate members. The initial members of the commission shall have
17 staggered terms. The members shall be residents of the City of Gloucester and shall be
18 knowledgeable of the fishing industry. The chairperson and vice chairperson shall be elected by
19 a vote of the majority of the members present and voting thereon.
20 SECTION 4. Said chapter 260 is hereby further amended by striking out Section 3A, as
21 amended by Section 2 of said Chapter 361 of the Acts of 1980, and inserting in place thereof the
22 following section:-- "Section 3A. The Mayor may appoint an executive director who shall
23 receive such compensation as determined by the mayor."
24 SECTION 5. This Act shall take effect upon its passage.
