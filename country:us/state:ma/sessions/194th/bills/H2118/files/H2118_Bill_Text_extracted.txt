Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 24L of Chapter 149 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in lines 104 and 105, the words “provided
3 that such consideration is specified in the noncompetition agreement” and inserting in place
4 thereof the following: - “provided that such agreement is reasonably related to the requirements
5 of the garden leave clause as set out in Section (i) of this subsection and provided further that
6 such consideration is specified in the noncompetition agreement.”
