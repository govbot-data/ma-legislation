HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 12, 2026.
The committee on Advanced Information Technology, the Internet and
Cybersecurity, to whom was referred the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 81) of Steven S. Howitt relative to artificial intelligence
disclosures; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 90) of Jay
