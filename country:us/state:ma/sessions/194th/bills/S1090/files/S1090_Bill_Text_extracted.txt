An Act relative to dangerousness hearings.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 58A of chapter 276, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended
2 by inserting after subsection (8) the following subsection:-
3 (9) If an individual is charged for the second time with an offense listed in subsection (1)
4 the commonwealth shall move, based on dangerousness, for an order of pretrial detention under
5 subsection (3) or release on conditions under subsection (2).
