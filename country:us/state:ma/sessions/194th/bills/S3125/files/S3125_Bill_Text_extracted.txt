An Act enhancing hospital emergency services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 51.25 of chapter 111 of the General Laws, as inserted by
2 section 11 of chapter 16 of the Acts of 2025, is hereby amended by striking out subsection (d)
3 and inserting in place thereof the following subsections:-
4 (d) The attorney general may bring a civil action for injunctive or other equitable
5 relief to enforce this section. In any action brought by the attorney general under this section, the
6 court may also award a civil penalty of not more than $50,000 for each violation. A hospital or
7 person engaging in gross, flagrant, or repetitive violations of this section shall be subject to
8 license revocation.
9 (e) Annually, not later than September 1, a hospital licensed under this chapter with an
10 emergency care department shall submit to the department a written report that includes the
11 hospital’s policies, procedures and processes for providing services consistent with this section.
12 (f) An individual who suffers personal harm as a direct result of a violation of a
13 requirement of this section may bring an action for damages in a court of competent jurisdiction.
