HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 9, 2026.
The committee on Transportation, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3720) of Kathleen R. LaNatra and
Michael D. Brady for legislation to require a minimum of 2 persons for the
movement of certain freight trains; and the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3721) of Kathleen R. LaNatra and Michael D. Brady relative
to railroad crew transportation, reports recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5135) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JAMES ARCIERO.
