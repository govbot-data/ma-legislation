An Act providing for the filling of a vacancy on the Groton-Dunstable School Committee.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding the Groton-Dunstable regional school district agreement,
2 section 16A of chapter 71 of the General Laws, or any other general or special law to the
3 contrary, the vacancy in one of the two Dunstable seats on the Groton-Dunstable regional school
4 district committee following the 2026 Dunstable annual town election may be filled by the
5 Dunstable select board and the remaining Dunstable member of the Groton-Dunstable regional
6 school district committee, acting jointly by majority vote, by appointing a member to serve a
7 three-year term until the 2029 Dunstable annual town election.
