An Act relative to clinical laboratory testing.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8 of chapter 111D of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting in line 22 after the words “upon the written
3 request of” the following:- the person from whose body the specimen was derived.
4 SECTION 2. Section 8 of Chapter 111D of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
5 Official Edition, is further amended by inserting in line 34 after the words “patients” the
6 following:-
7 A laboratory test ordered under this section, regardless of test complexity, as defined
8 under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988, shall be conducted by a
9 laboratory that routinely participates in proficiency testing for such offered test in a program
10 approved by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services. This paragraph shall not be
11 construed to mandate proficiency testing for a laboratory test if it is not available from any
12 approved program.
