Conflict Resolution: A comprehensive 4-month cognitive behavioral
program designed to address criminal behavior, anger management, conflict,
and victim impact. The objective is to help identify patterns, choices, and
identify thinking errors. The goal is to assist them in finding alternative
behaviors, healthy responses, emotional regulation, and reducing
consequences.
Program Manager Sandra Crispin.
• 14 new participants began the program this quarter, and 1 graduated. 0
program failure for discipline, 1 non-disciplinary discharges. The
average number of participants each month in the program was 9
• A second Conflict Resolution program began in June. The program is for
the trial population and 9 participants signed up.
Making Changes Program: 3-month Reentry preparation and life skills-based
program. Ideally participants join this program 6 to 9 months prior to their
release. The goal is discharging planning and identifying community
resources to support each individual reentry plan and needs.
Reentry staff coordinator Mollie Simmons.
• 9 participants began the program, 2 graduated from the program, 2
program failures for disciplinary reasons, 3 discharged non-disciplinary.
The average number of participants each month in the program for the
quarter is 12.
