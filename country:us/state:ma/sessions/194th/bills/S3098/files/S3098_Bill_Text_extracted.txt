An Act authorizing the town of Dennis to publish legal notices digitally.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding subsection (b) of section 13 of chapter 4 of the General
2 Laws or any other general or special law to the contrary, whenever any officer, department,
3 board, committee, or commission of the town of Dennis is to publish a legal notice in a
4 newspaper or newspaper of general circulation, as the case may be, such requirement may be
5 satisfied by one or more of the following means:
6 (a) a newspaper or newspaper of general circulation’s print publication;
7 (b) a newspaper or newspaper of general circulation’s website;
8 (c) websites reporting local news and opinion which satisfy all criteria for digital
9 publication set forth in subsection (c) of said section 13;
10 (d) a website that may be maintained as a statewide repository for such notices; or
1 of 2

11 (e) a town of Dennis website that may be maintained as a repository for such notices.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
