HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 10, 2025.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom were referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3318)
of Mindy Domb relative to menstrual products in state buildings; and the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3370) of Meghan K. Kilcoyne
and Jacob R. Oliveira relative to additional work in contracts of the
Division of Highways, reports recommending that the accompanying
order (House, No. 4714) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.
