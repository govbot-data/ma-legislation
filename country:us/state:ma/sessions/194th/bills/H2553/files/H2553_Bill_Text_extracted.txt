An Act ensuring access to medical records.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 111 of the General Laws is hereby amended after section 244 by adding the
2 following section:
3 Section 255. (a) No entity shall charge a separate fee for the transmission of data from
4 patient electronic medical records when necessary to facilitate coverage, payment for services,
5 care coordination or health care quality improvement efforts.
