An Act relative to a cranberry water use transfer program.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 21G of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the
2 following section:-
3 Section 21. Registration statements and permits for withdrawals for use in a cranberry
4 operation may be transferred to and used for mitigation by other permitted or registered users.
5 SECTION 2. Not later than December 31, 2026, the department of environmental
6 protection shall establish such regulations, principles, policies and guidelines as are necessary to
7 implement section 21 of chapter 21G of the General Laws.
