HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 19, 2026.
The committee on Election Laws, to whom was referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 808) of Tackey Chan relative to
requiring residency affidavits for candidates for public office, reports
(under the provisions of House Rule 27) recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5251) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
DANIEL J. HUNT.
