RE: Department of Youth Services’ Annual Report, pursuant to M.G.L. c. 123A, §16
Dear Mr. Hurley and Mr. Carroll:
Pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws c. 123A, §16, the Department of Youth Services (DYS)
is required to report annually to the Legislature regarding the treatment offered to each person
who has been committed to its custody as a sexually dangerous person and, without disclosing
the identity of such persons, describe the treatment provided.
DYS reports to the Legislature that it has not had anyone committed to its custody as a sexually
dangerous person during this reporting period.
Sincerely yours,
