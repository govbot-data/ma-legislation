HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 16, 2026.
The committee on Public Health, to whom was referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 453) of Edward R. Philips relative to the
licensure of dietitians and nutritionists, reports recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5233) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
MARJORIE C. DECKER.

HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5233
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, March 16, 2026.
1 Ordered, That the committee on Public Health be authorized to sit during a recess of the
2 General Court to make an investigation and study of House document numbered 453, relative to
3 licensure of dietitians and nutritionists.
4 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
5 and its recommendations, if any, together with drafts of legislation necessary to carry such
6 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
7 on or before December 31, 2026.
