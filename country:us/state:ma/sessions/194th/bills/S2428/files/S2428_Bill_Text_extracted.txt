An Act relative to handicap parking access for individuals with autism.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The ninth paragraph of section 2 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the
2 2014 Official Edition, by inserting after the first sentence the following:-
3 An individual diagnosed with 1 of the autism spectrum disorders or the parent or
4 guardian of an individual diagnosed with 1 of the autism spectrum disorders may be issued a
5 placard, provided that the parent or guardian shall only use such placard when transporting the
6 individual diagnosed with 1 of the autism spectrum disorders.
