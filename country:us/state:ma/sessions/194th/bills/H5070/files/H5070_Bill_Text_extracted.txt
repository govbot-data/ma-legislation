HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 17, 2026.
The committee on Labor and Workforce Development, to whom were
referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2077) of Joanie
Lawlor for legislation to require certain employers to establish and maintain
a human resources department; the petition (accompanied by bill, House,
No. 2132) of Bradley H. Jones, Jr. and others for legislation to establish a
special commission (including members of the General Court) to study
issues relative to commission sales rates; the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 2135) of Bradley H. Jones, Jr. and others relative to minors
engaged in the work of vlogging; and the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 2183) of Erika Uyterhoeven, Jason M. Lewis and Carmine
Lawrence Gentile for legislation to ban captive audience and employer
sponsored meetings, reports recommending that the accompanying order
(House, No. 5070) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
PAUL MCMURTRY.
