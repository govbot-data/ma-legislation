An Act to stabilize adult day health services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 118 E, Section 13C is hereby amended by inserting at the end
2 thereof the following:
3 The secretary of the executive office shall have the responsibility for establishing rates of
4 payment for by efficiently and economically operated Adult Day Health Service providers in
5 providing Adult Day Health Services in conformity with federal and state law, regulations and
6 quality and safety standards; provided, that the secretary may designate another governmental
7 unit to perform such ratemaking functions.
8 When establishing rates of payment for Adult Day Health Services, the secretary of the
9 executive office shall adjust rates to take into account factors, including, but not limited to:
1 of 3

10 (i) the reasonable cost to Adult Day Health Service providers of any existing or new
11 governmental mandate that has been enacted, promulgated or imposed by any governmental unit
12 or federal governmental authority;
13 (ii) a cost adjustment actor, to reflect changes in reasonable costs of goods, services, and
14 transportation, of Adult Day Health programs including those attributed to wages and benefits,
15 which shall not be less than the HCP CPI;
16 (iii) establishing wages and benefits comparable to direct care staff in other health care
17 settings;
18 (iv) individual ADH Provider differences in real estate and capital costs, including rent,
19 furniture, fixtures, and equipment, and any other capital costs.
20 SECTION 2. Chapter 118E, Section 13D is amended after the second paragraph by
21 inserting the following:
22 Such rates for Adult Day Services shall be established as of July 1 biennially.

24 following:
