An Act relative to the maximum age requirement for original appointment as a police officer for
the city of Worcester.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding section 58 of chapter 31 of the General Laws or any other
2 general or special law to the contrary, no person shall be eligible to have their name certified for
3 original appointment to the position of police officer in the city of Worcester if such person has
4 reached their fortieth birthday on the date of the entrance examination.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
