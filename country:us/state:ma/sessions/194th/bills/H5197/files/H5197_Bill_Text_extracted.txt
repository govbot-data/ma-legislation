HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 9, 2026.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom was referred so much of the recommendations of the Inspector
General (House, No. 11) as relates to electric vehicles and charging
infrastructure and Chapter 30B, the Uniform Procurement Act (House, No.
14), reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5197)
ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.
