Unified Carrier Registration Trust Fund Report Page 3
Department of Public Utilities Unified Carrier Registration Trust Fund
Between November 22, 2023, and November 6, 2024, the Department of Public Utilities collected
$1,643,822.75 in revenue and expended $2,730,963.06 from the Unified Carrier Registration Trust
Fund, starting with the FY2023 reported balance of $1,782,284.68. The Unified Carrier
Registration Trust Fund currently has an unexpended balance of $695,144.37.
Previously Reported Credited Spent Unexpended
Balance
$1,782,284.68 $1,643,822.75 $2,730,963.06 $695,144.37
The spending from the Unified Carrier Registration Trust is to fund the payroll and
administration costs, including vehicle, office supplies, training, travel, and reimbursements for
fourteen full-time staff to administer the Federal Unified Carrier Registration program, as well as
enforcement activities in conjunction with the Massachusetts State Police.
