SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 171
By Ms. Rausch, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 171) of Rebecca L. Rausch for
legislation to support maternal health by providing financial assistance to an eligible pregnant
person. Children, Families and Persons with Disabilities.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to support maternal health.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the department of transitional
2 assistance shall provide financial assistance to an otherwise eligible pregnant person with no
3 dependent child beginning when the pregnancy is verified; provided, however, that such
4 assistance shall be the amount for one person under the transitional aid to families with
5 dependent children program.
