An Act relative to rodeos.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 128 is hereby amended by inserting, after section 2B, a new section:-
2 Section 2B1/2. Rodeos
3 Rodeos shall comply with the rules, regulations, standards, and practices established by
4 the Professional Rodeo Cowboy Association. For the purposes of this section, “rodeo” shall be
5 defined as “a competition or exhibition in which individuals compete by or showcase skills
6 associated with traditional cowboy activities, including but not limited to bull riding, bronc
7 riding, bareback bronc riding, steer wrestling, calf roping, and team roping, and include the use
8 of animals, including but not limited to bulls, steers, calves, broncs, and horses”.
