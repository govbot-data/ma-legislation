HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 2, 2026.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom was referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3397) of
Tram T. Nguyen, Steven J. Ouellette and others for legislation to designate
the blue-spotted salamander as the official amphibian of the
Commonwealth, reports recommending that the accompanying order
(House, No. 5170) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.
