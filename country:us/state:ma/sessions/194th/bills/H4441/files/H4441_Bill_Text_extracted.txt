An Act amending the charter for the town of Nantucket.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 2.2 of article II of the charter of the town of Nantucket, as
2 established by chapter 289 of the acts of 1996, as amended, which is on file in the office of the
3 archivist of the commonwealth, as provided in section 12 of chapter 43B of the General Laws, is
4 hereby amended by striking out the words “one year” and inserting in place thereof the following
5 words:- three years.
6 SECTION 2. Section 2.4 of said article II of said charter is hereby amended by inserting
7 after the words “Moderator shall appoint a clerk” the following words:- , who shall be a
8 registered voter of the town.

10 amended by inserting after the words “after the issuance of the warrant” the following words:-
11 and make a copy of the warrant available on the town website.
12 SECTION 4. Subsection (c) of said section 2.5 of said article II of said charter is hereby
13 amended by striking out the word “seven” and inserting in place thereof the following word:-
14 fourteen.
15 SECTION 5. Said section 2.5 of said article II of said charter is hereby amended by
16 inserting after subsection (c) the following new subsection:- (d) Notwithstanding any general or
17 special law to the contrary, the Select Board shall insert in the warrant for the annual meeting all
18 subjects the insertion of which has been requested of them in writing by fifty or more registered
19 voters of the town and in the warrant for every special town meeting all subjects the insertion of
20 which has been requested of them in writing by one hundred registered voters.
21 SECTION 6. Section 2.6 of said article II of said charter is hereby amended by inserting
22 at the end of said section the following words:- The Moderator, in his or her sole discretion, may
23 permit additional nonresident property owners to speak at any annual or special Town Meeting
24 on matters before the Meeting.

29 amended by inserting after the words “shall appoint the” the following word:- department.
