An Act relative to charter school expansion.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 89 of chapter 71 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (ll) the following subsection:-
3 (ll1/2) If the department rejects a charter school’s request to amend its charter for the
4 purposes of expanding the number of school districts specified in said charter, the department
5 shall require a 3 year waiting period before the charter school may file another request to amend
6 its charter for the purposes of expanding the number of school districts specified in said charter.
