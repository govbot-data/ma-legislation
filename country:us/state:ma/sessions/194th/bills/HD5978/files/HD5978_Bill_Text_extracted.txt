An Act relative to disability retirement income.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 91A chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the figure “$15,000”, in line 27, the
3 following words:- from the date of retirement until 10 years following the date of retirement and
4 $75,000 for more than 10 years following the date of retirement until 20 years following the date
5 of retirement.
6 SECTION 2. Said section 91A of said chapter 32, as so appearing, is hereby further
7 amended by inserting after the word “therefor”, in lines 30 and 31, the following words:- ;
8 provided, that no such cap shall apply if more than 20 years have passed since the date of
9 retirement.
