An Act defining "Gold Star families".
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 7 of chapter 4 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting the following definitions under the forty third definition:-
3 "Gold Star parent" shall mean any parent of a member of the armed forces whose death
4 occurred as a result of injury sustained, illness, or disease contracted, not due to gross negligence
5 or misconduct of the member, during active service.
6 "Gold Star spouse" any spouse of a member of the armed forces whose death occurred as
7 a result of injury sustained, illness, or disease contracted, not due to gross negligence or
8 misconduct of the member, during active service.
