An Act relative to restrictions on highway breakdown lane travel.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 85 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following section: -
2 Section 38. (a) For the purposes of this section, the words “breakdown lane” shall mean a
3 paved lane on a way to the right of the travel lanes and separated from them by a solid line.
4 (b) All highway breakdown lanes designated by signs for travel use during certain
5 allowed times shall have a maximum speed of 30 mph. An operator of a motor vehicle who
6 commits a speeding violation in a breakdown lane shall be punished by a fine of $100.
7 (c) An operator of a motor vehicle traveling in a highway breakdown lane shall, upon
8 entering the breakdown lane, exit the breakdown lane at the next available exit ramp. An
9 operator of a motor vehicle who does not exit a breakdown lane at the next available exit ramp
10 shall be punished by a fine of $100.
