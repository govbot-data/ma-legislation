HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5063
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to designate the courthouse in the Roxbury section of the city of Boston as the Edward
O. Gourdin Courthouse.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The Roxbury Division Courthouse of the Boston Municipal Court shall be designated and
2 known as the Edward O. Gourdin Courthouse, in memory of the late honorable Edward Orval
3 “Ned” Gourdin for his many contributions to the judiciary, his community and the
4 commonwealth. The division of capital asset management and maintenance shall erect and
5 maintain suitable markers bearing the designation in compliance with any applicable standards.
