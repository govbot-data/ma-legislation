Dear Committees on Ways and Means:
In accordance with Section 2E of Chapter 9 of the Acts of 2025, I am submitting a Fiscal Year
2026 transfer schedule for the Clean Energy Investment Fund at the Massachusetts Clean Energy
Technology Center.
• 1595-6232 -- Clean Energy Investment Fund, established in section 15 of said chapter 23J of
the General Laws, for clean energy workforce development initiatives and investments to
support emissions reductions in the energy, transportation and buildings sectors, including
investments to support entrepreneurial fellowships enabling the translation of research into
businesses, products and jobs in the energy, transportation and buildings sectors. [NOTE:
Although the General Appropriation Act continues to reference the Clean Energy Investment
Fund, Chapter 238 of the Acts of 2024 changed the name of that fund to the Climatetech
Investment Fund.]
Monthly transfer amounts are shown in the attachment.
I will notify you if any changes are made to the schedule. If you have any questions or concerns,
please contact me.
Sincerely,
William McNamara
