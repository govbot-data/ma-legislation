An Act relative to the definition of electric mopeds.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the definition of “Electric bicycle” the
3 following definition:-
4 “Electric moped”, a pedal bicycle that has a helper motor, or a non-pedal bicycle that has
5 a motor, with an electric motor of no more than 2 horsepower or 1500 watts, an automatic
6 transmission and that is capable of a maximum speed of no more than 30 miles per hour.
7 SECTION 2. Said section 1 of said chapter 90, as so appearing, is hereby further
8 amended by striking out the definition of “Motorized bicycle” and inserting the following
9 definition:-
10 “Motorized bicycle”, (i) a pedal bicycle which has a helper motor, or a non-pedal bicycle
11 which has a motor, with a cylinder capacity not exceeding 50 cubic centimeters, an automatic
12 transmission and which is capable of a maximum speed of no more than 30 miles per hour; or (ii)
1 of 2

13 an electric moped; provided, that the definition of ''motorized bicycle'' shall not include an
14 electric bicycle.
