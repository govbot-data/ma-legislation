An Act establishing a local value added tax on cannabis cultivation sales.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 94G of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by striking out subsection (d) of section 3 in its entirety and inserting
3 in its place thereof the following:-
4 (d) No municipality shall require a host community agreement (HCA) as a condition for
5 the licensing, operation, or approval of any cannabis cultivation establishment within its
6 jurisdiction. Cannabis cultivators shall be subject to all applicable state laws and regulations but
7 shall not be required to enter into separate agreements with municipalities beyond the
8 requirements of state law.
9 SECTION 2. Chapter 64H of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
10 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting the following:-
11 Section 6B. Local Value Added Tax on Cannabis Cultivation Sales
1 of 2

12 (a) In addition to the state sales tax imposed under section 2 of chapter 64H, there shall
13 be an additional local value added tax (VAT) of 1% on the sale of completed cannabis goods by
14 a licensed cannabis cultivation facility to licensed cannabis retailers in any municipality within
15 the commonwealth. The tax shall be calculated based on the value added to the goods during the
16 cultivation process.
17 (b) The local value added tax shall be collected by the cultivator at the point of sale or
18 transfer of completed goods and remitted to the department of revenue, which shall distribute the
19 funds to the appropriate municipality.
20 (c) The 1% local VAT shall be in addition to any local sales tax and shall be applied to
21 the value of completed cannabis products sold as adult-use cannabis products. Medical marijuana
22 products shall be exempt.
23 SECTION 3. The department of revenue shall promulgate regulations regarding VAT
24 collection and distribution within 90 days of the effective date of this act.
