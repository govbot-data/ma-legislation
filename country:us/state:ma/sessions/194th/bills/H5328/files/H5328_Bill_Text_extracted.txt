HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, April 9, 2026.
The committee on Transportation, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3636) of Michelle L. Ciccolo for
legislation to allow municipalities to finance expanded transit options; the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3669) of Jessica Ann Giannino
for legislation to further regulate the operation of autonomous vehicles;
and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4166) of Michelle L.
Ciccolo relative to climate adaptation and transportation infrastructure,
reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5328)
ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JAMES ARCIERO.
