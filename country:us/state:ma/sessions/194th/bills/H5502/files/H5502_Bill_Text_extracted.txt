An Act relative to school choice.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 12B of chapter 76 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting, in line 216, after the word “sections” the following words:- ;
3 provided, however, that any municipal school district operating pursuant to a tuition agreement,
4 and not a regional school district agreement, for the provision of middle and high school
5 education, may exempt incoming school choice students at the elementary level from
6 participation in such tuition agreement.
