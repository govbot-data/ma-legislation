In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court

_______________
An Act relative to equal state tax treatment for foreign Social Security-equivalent benefits.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Paragraph (2) of subsection (a) of section 2 of chapter 62 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2024 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out subparagraph (H) and
3 inserting in place thereof the following subparagraph:-
4 (H) Social security benefits included in federal gross income under section 86 of the
5 Code and foreign government pension benefits that are the equivalent of United States Social
6 Security benefits, as recognized by a reciprocal tax treaty between the United States and a
7 foreign country.
