Resolve establishing a special commission to investigate and study the protection of junior
operators.
1 Resolved, there is hereby established a special commission to study laws and regulations
2 relevant to junior operator licenses and vehicle safety for minors with the goal of increasing
3 driver safety and reducing instances of traffic fatalities. Topics of consideration shall include, but
4 not be limited to, (i) further deterrents from operating a vehicle unlawfully, (ii) prohibiting junior
5 operators from carrying passengers in vehicles, (iii) increasing penalties for roadway violations,
6 (iv) additional driver’s education and training hours, and (v) continuing education for junior
7 The special commission shall consist of: the secretary of the executive office of public
8 safety and security, or their designee, who shall serve as chair; the secretary of the department of
9 transportation, or their designee; the registrar of motor vehicles, or their designee; the president
10 of the senate, or their designee; the minority leader of the senate, or their designee; the speaker of
1 of 2

11 the house of representatives, or their designee; the minority leader of the house of
12 representatives, or their designee; and an instructor of a drivers education program, to be
13 appointed by the Governor.
14 The commission shall report their findings, along with any recommendations for
15 legislation, to the chairs of the joint committee on public safety and homeland security no later
16 than December 1, 2026.
