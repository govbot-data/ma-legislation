An Act authorizing the town of Colrain to continue the employment of fire department chief
Nicholas Anzuoni.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding chapter 32 of the General Laws or any general or special
2 law to the contrary, Nicholas Anzuoni, chief of the Colrain fire department, may continue to
3 serve in such position until they reach the age of 70, the date of their retirement, whichever
4 occurs first; provided, however, that they remain physically and mentally capable of performing
5 the duties of the position; provided further, that Nicholas Anzuoni may require, at the expense of
6 the town of Colrain, be examined by an impartial physician designated by the town to determine
7 such capability. This act shall not entitle Nicholas Anzuoni to remain in service to the town of
8 Colrain if otherwise removed or suspended in accordance with applicable laws, rules, and
9 regulations.
10 SECTION 2. No deductions shall be made from the regular compensation of Nicholas
11 Anzuoni pursuant to chapter 32 of the General Laws for any service performed subsequent to his
1 of 2

12 reaching the age of 65. Upon his retirement they shall receive a superannuation retirement
13 allowance equal to that which they would have been entitled had they retired upon reaching the
14 age of 65, if any.
15 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
