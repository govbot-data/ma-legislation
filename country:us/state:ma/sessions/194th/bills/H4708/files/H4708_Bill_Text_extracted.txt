The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 10, 2025.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3104) of Colleen M. Garry relative to
making assisted living programs eligible for tax increment financing and
allowing municipalities to provide flexible targeted incentives; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3124) of Natalie M. Higgins and others
relative to a sales tax exemption for the sale of used books and other items
sold by libraries for fundraising purposes; and the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 3125) of Kate Hogan relative to privately owned public use
airport real estate taxes, reports recommending that the accompanying order
(House, No. 4708) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
