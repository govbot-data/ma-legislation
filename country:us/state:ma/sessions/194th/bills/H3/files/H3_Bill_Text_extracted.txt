Dear Clerk James,
In accordance with Section 33 of Chapter 30 of the General Laws, which requires that "[s]uch
recommendations shall be filed with the clerk of the house of representatives on or before the first
Wednesday in November preceding the first annual session of the general court,” I write to file with you
several bills for consideration in the 194th General Court:
An Act to reform payments in lieu of taxes for state-owned land
An Act to improve oversight of state government
An Act improving government accountability
An Act improving the internal controls within state agencies
An Act creating an electronic backpack for foster children
An Act to improve transportation and environmental oversight
An Act relative to nondisclosure agreements
