Dear Committees on Ways and Means:
In accordance with Section 2E of Chapter 9 of the Acts of 2025, I am submitting a Fiscal Year
2026 transfer schedule for the Childhood Lead Poisoning Prevention Trust Fund.
• 1595-4506 -- Childhood Lead Poisoning Prevention Trust Fund, where the transfer provides
an operating subsidy from the General Fund to the Childhood Lead Poisoning Prevention
Trust Fund for the operation of the childhood lead poisoning prevention program, including
the production and dissemination of educational and other materials pertaining to lead paint
poisoning prevention and treatment; training of lead paint inspectors, and training of
homeowners in those aspects of lead paint abatement or containment.
Monthly transfer amounts are shown in the attachment.
I will notify you if any changes are made to the schedule. If you have any questions or concerns,
please contact me.
Sincerely,
William McNamara
Comptroller of the Commonwealth
