_______________

_______________
An Act further regulating the amendment of a conservation restriction in the town of Hanson.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 133 of the acts of 2022 is hereby amended by striking out section 3
2 and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
3 Section 3. As consideration for the change in the conservation restriction authorized in
4 section 1, the town of Hanson shall, pursuant to section 3 of chapter 40 of the General Laws,
5 transfer the care, custody and control of a certain parcel of land consisting of 25.403 acres, more
6 or less, and identified on the town of Hanson assessors’ map as map 49, parcel 1-1A and a parcel
7 of land consisting of 3,922 square feet, more or less, and identified as assessors’ map 105, parcel
8 6 from the board of selectmen to the conservation commission for the purpose of wildlife,
9 recreation and forestry.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
