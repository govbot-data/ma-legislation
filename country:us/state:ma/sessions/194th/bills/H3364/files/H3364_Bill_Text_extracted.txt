An Act relative to online posting of CMRs.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 30 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting the following new section:-
3 Section 66. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, each agency
4 within the Executive branch must post all relevant Code of Massachusetts Regulations (CMR) on
5 the Executive agency’s website. Each agency must further update said regulations by June 30 of
6 each year.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective on July 1, 2027.
