SENATE DOCKET, NO. 3555 FILED ON: 1/7/2026
Senate, January 7, 2026 -- (Filed by Ms. Rausch) Order relative to the daily printing of the
Journal of the Senate.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
Senate, January 07, 2026.
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
1 Ordered, That, the Clerk begin the daily printing of the Journal of the Senate, as
2 authorized by Senate Rule 6, and that the daily reading thereof be dispensed with; that, under
3 authority of Section 22A of Chapter 3 of the General Laws, copies of the Journals of the entire
4 session be printed and bound with the customary appendices and an index; and that a certified
5 copy thereof be deposited with the Secretary of the Commonwealth as the official bound Journal
6 of the Senate.
