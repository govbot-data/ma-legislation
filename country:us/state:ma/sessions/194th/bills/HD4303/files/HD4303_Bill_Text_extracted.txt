57 50% of that member’s additional regular compensation for the second year of a biennial session

59 (i)(1) On the third Monday in December of the first year of a biennial session of the
60 general court, the clerk of the senate and the clerk of the house of representatives shall jointly
61 examine the records of each committee the chairs of which are eligible to be paid additional
62 regular compensation pursuant to subsection (d) of this section. For each such committee, if the
63 records document that as of that date the committee has held a public hearing and a public mark-
64 up session on each bill referred to it before November 1 of such year and that every report filed
65 by the committee has been approved by a vote of a majority of the members of the committee at
66 a public meeting at which a quorum was present, the clerk of the senate and the clerk of the
67 house of representatives shall so certify jointly. Such certification shall constitute the
68 certification required pursuant to subsection (h)(1) of this section to pay additional regular
69 compensation pursuant to subsection (d) of this section.
70 (2) On the last Friday in May of the second year of a biennial session of the general court,
71 the clerk of the senate and the clerk of the house of representatives shall jointly examine the
72 records of committees the chairs of which are eligible to be paid additional regular compensation
73 pursuant to subsection (d) of this section. For each such committee, if the records document that
74 the committee has held a public hearing and a public mark-up session on each bill referred to it

76 majority of the members of the committee at a public meeting at which a quorum was present,
77 the clerk of the senate and the clerk of the house of representatives shall so certify jointly. Such

79 to pay additional regular compensation pursuant to subsection (d) of this section.
