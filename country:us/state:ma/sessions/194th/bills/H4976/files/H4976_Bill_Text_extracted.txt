An Act relative to littering.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after Section 22G the following section:-
3 Section 22G 1/2. No operator of a motor vehicle shall litter, or knowingly permit an
4 occupant of their vehicle to litter on public or private property through the disposal of trash or
5 garbage from said motor vehicle. A violation of this section shall be considered a civil motor
6 vehicle infraction and shall be punishable by a civil citation issued pursuant to chapter 90C,
7 which shall include a fine of $100 for a first offense, $250 for a second offense, and $500 for a
8 third or subsequent offense. A civil citation issued for a third or subsequent offense shall be
9 considered a surchargeable offense under Section 113B of Chapter 175.
10 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect upon its passage.
