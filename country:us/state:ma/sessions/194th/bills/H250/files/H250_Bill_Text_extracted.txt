An Act relative to foster care recruitment.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, annually, not
2 later than October 31, the department of children and families shall submit a special report on its
3 foster care campaign to recruit new foster parents to the child advocate, the clerks of the senate
4 and house of representatives, the house and senate committees on ways and means and the joint
5 committee on children, families, and persons with disabilities. The report shall be made available
6 to the public electronically in accordance with section 19 of chapter 66. The report shall include,
7 but not be limited to, the: (i) number of new foster care parents as a result of the foster care
8 campaign; (ii) the costs associated with such a recruitment effort; (iii) difficulties impeding the
9 department's ability to recruit additional foster parents; and (ii) methods in which the department
1 of 2

10 is recruiting foster care parents. The department may satisfy the reporting requirements of this
11 section by providing the requested information in an annual report filed.
12 SECTION 2. Section 1 is hereby repealed.
13 SECTION 3. Section 2 shall take effect on December 31, 2026.
