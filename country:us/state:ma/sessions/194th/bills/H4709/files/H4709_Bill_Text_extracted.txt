HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 10, 2025.
The committee on Revenue, to whom was referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3157) of Patrick Joseph Kearney relative
to repealing the sales tax on boats built or rebuilt in the Commonwealth,
reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 4709)
ought to be adopted [Representative Lombardo of Billerica dissents].
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
