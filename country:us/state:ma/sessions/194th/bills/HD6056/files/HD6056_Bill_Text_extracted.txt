Annual Report on MBTA Bus Lane Enforcement Violations
Pursuant to Section 8 of Chapter 90K of the Massachusetts General Laws, the Massachusetts Bay
Transportation Authority (MBTA) submits this report concerning camera enforceable violations for calendar
year 2025.
As authorized under Section 8 of Chapter 90K of the Massachusetts General Laws, and the MBTA’s
implementing regulation, 703 CMR 6.00, the MBTA is preparing to deploy an automated camera system for
detecting motor vehicles unlawfully stopped or parked in bus stops and bus lanes. The statute requires the
MBTA to publish an annual report no later than March 1 that includes, but is not limited to, data on citations
issued (number and location) as well as consideration of the social and racial equity impacts of implementing
Chapter 90K over the preceding year.
Annual Report Metrics
The MBTA issued 0 citations for camera enforceable violations in bus stops and bus lanes for the period from
January 1, 2025, through December 31, 2025. Consequently, there are no equity impacts of citations issued
during the reporting period.
