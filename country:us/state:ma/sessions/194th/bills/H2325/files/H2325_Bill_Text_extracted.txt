An Act relative to ice cream truck vending.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 25 of chapter 270 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “truck”, in line 15, the following
3 words:- ; provided, however, that a person under the age of 21 may engage in ice cream truck
4 vending without a valid permit if they are working under the direct supervision of a person who
5 has been issued a valid permit.
6 SECTION 2. Said section 25 of said chapter 270, as so appearing, is hereby further
7 amended by striking out, in line 31, the word “and”.
8 SECTION 3. Said section 25 of said chapter 270, as so appearing, is hereby further
9 amended by striking out, in line 34, the words “General Laws.” and inserting in place thereof the
10 following words:- General Laws; and.
11 SECTION 4. Subsection (c) of said section 25 of said chapter 270, as so appearing, is
12 hereby amended by adding the following clause:-
1 of 2

13 (v) a requirement that a valid permit issued by a permitting authority shall be accepted by
14 the permitting authorities in other municipalities without a separate permit application.
