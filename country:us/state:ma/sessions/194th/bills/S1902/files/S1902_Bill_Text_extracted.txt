An Act relative to surviving family members of public emergency medical technicians.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 100 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the word “death,” in line 15 the following
3 words:-
4 or if a public emergency medical technician while in the performance of his duties and as
5 the result of an assault on his person or as a result of an accident while responding to or returning
6 from any emergency or as the result of an accident involving an EMS department vehicle, which
7 the public emergency medical technician is operating or in which he is riding or while at the
8 scene of any emergency is killed or sustains injuries while on duty which results in his death,
