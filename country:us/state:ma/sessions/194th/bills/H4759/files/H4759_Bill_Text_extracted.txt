An Act relative to modernizing Chapter 40R.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 9 of chapter 40R of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
2 amended by striking out subsections (a) and (b) and inserting in place thereof the following 2
3 subsections:-
4 (a) The commonwealth shall pay from the trust fund or other funds from appropriations
5 or other money authorized by the general court a zoning incentive payment, according to the
6 following schedule:
7 Projected Units of New Construction% Payment
8 Up to 20% $20,000
9 21 to 100% $150,000
10 101 to 200% $400,000
11 201 to 500% $740,000

12 501 or more% $1,200,000
13 Subject to any conditions imposed by the department as a condition of approving a smart
14 growth zoning district or starter home zoning district, the zoning incentive payment shall be
15 payable upon confirmation of approval of the district by the department. The projected number
16 of units shall be based upon the zoning adopted in the smart growth zoning district or starter
17 home zoning district.
18 (b) The commonwealth shall pay from the trust fund or other funds from appropriations
19 or other money authorized by the general court a 1-time density bonus payment to each city or
20 town with an approved smart growth zoning district and a 1-time production bonus payment to
21 each city or town with an approved starter home zoning district. This payment shall be $6,000
22 for each housing unit of new construction created in the smart growth zoning district and $6,000
23 for each housing unit of new construction created in the starter home zoning district. The amount
24 due shall be paid on a unit-by-unit basis in accordance with department regulations, upon
25 submission by a city or town of proof of issuance of a building permit for a particular housing
26 unit or units within the district.”
27 SECTION 2. Section 12 of Chapter 40R of the General Laws is hereby amended by
28 inserting in line 3, after the word “review”, the following words:-
29 “, including but not limited to an analysis of the incentive payments, in Section 9, to
30 determine that those payments are consistent with housing costs per the Consumer Price Index.”
31 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect for tax years beginning on or after January 1,
32 2026.
