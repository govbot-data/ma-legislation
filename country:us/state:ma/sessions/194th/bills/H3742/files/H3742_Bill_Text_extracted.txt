An Act promoting personal access of driving records.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1A of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2014 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:- The registrar shall make a person’s
3 driving records available online for informational purposes. Such records shall be considered
4 unattested and need not be certified with the signature of the registrar, but shall include any
5 suspensions, outstanding tickets or other citations issued to the driver. Such records shall be
6 updated regularly to reflect the most accurate information available, consistent with the way in
7 which attested driving records are maintained for law enforcement agencies.
