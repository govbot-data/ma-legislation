The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 9, 2026.
The committee on Transportation, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3626) of David Biele for legislation to
authorize the MBTA to establish a reduced fare program for veterans; the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3635) of Tackey Chan relative
to establishing toll discounts on the Massachusetts Turnpike, Ted
Williams Tunnel and the Tobin Bridge for persons sixty-five years of age
or older; the joint petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3646) of
Leigh Davis for legislation to establish the quarterly allocation of Chapter
90 funds for cities and towns; and the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3647) of Leigh Davis relative to expenditures for maintaining,
repairing, improving, and constructing municipal ways and bridges and
other eligible municipal projects, reports recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5134) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JAMES ARCIERO.
