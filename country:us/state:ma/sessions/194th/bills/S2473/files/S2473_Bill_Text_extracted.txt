By Mr. Lewis, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 2473) of Jason M. Lewis for
legislation to ensure the safety of the Commonwealth by not authorizing any part of the
organized militia or national guard personnel on state active duty. Veterans and Federal Affairs.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to ensuring the safety of the Commonwealth.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 33 is hereby amended by striking out section 42 as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
3 Section 42. the commander in chief shall not order or authorize any part of the organized
4 militia or national guard personnel on state active duty to violate the Constitution of the
5 Commonwealth of Massachusetts or any law of the Commonwealth.
