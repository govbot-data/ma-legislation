An Act authorizing the town of Holbrook to appoint Patrick Doherty as a firefighter
notwithstanding the maximum age requirement.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding section 58A of chapter 31 of the General Laws or any
2 other general or special law to the contrary, the Town of Holbrook may appoint Patrick Doherty
3 as a firefighter in the Holbrook Fire Department, provided that he satisfies all other requirements
4 for appointment as determined by the Town of Holbrook, the Human Resources Division, the
5 Civil Service Commission, and any other applicable authority.
6 SECTION 2. Except as otherwise provided herein, Patrick Doherty shall be subject to all
7 laws, rules, regulations, policies, procedures, qualifications and standards applicable to
8 firefighters employed by the Town of Holbrook.
9 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
