Dear Mr. Clerks:
Pursuant to Section 18 of Chapter 94G of the Massachusetts General Laws, the Cannabis Control
Commission (Commission) is required to file an annual report with your offices including (i) the
number of audits performed under this section; (ii) a summary of findings under the audits; and
(iii) the cost of each audit.
Pursuant to statutory requirements, all audits shall be conducted in accordance with generally
accepted auditing standards established by the American Institute of Certified Public
Accountants. As of this date, the Commission has not conducted such audits because the
Commission requires additional funding to procure and/or pay the vendor with the relevant
expertise and experience in directing the specific audit allowed under current statute.
The Commission has accordingly requested an additional $500,000 in its FY2027 budget to
support the procurement of a qualified vendor with the requisite expertise to conduct these audits
in compliance with statutory requirements. Without this funding, the Commission will not be
able to complete this audit in FY2027.
Please let me know if you have any questions.
