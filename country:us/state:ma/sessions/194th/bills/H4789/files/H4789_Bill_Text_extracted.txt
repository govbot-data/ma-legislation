HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 9, 2025.
The committee on Financial Services, to whom were referred the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1155) of Michael J. Finn and
others relative to empowering health care consumers by further regulating
policies, contracts, agreements, plans or certificates of insurance; the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1325) of Alan Silvia relative to
access to generic medications; and the joint petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 4043) of Manny Cruz relative to copay assistance for
medications, reports recommending that the accompanying order (House,
No. 4789) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JAMES M. MURPHY.
