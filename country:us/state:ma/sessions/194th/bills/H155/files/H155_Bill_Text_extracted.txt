By Representative Jones of North Reading, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 155) of

The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing a special legislative commission to study the practice of lab shopping in the
cannabis industry.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. There shall be established a special legislative commission to study the
2 practice of lab shopping in the cannabis industry. The commission’s study shall include, but not
3 be limited to, the frequency, risks, and processes used by cultivators practicing lab shopping.
4 SECTION 2. The commission shall include: the chairs of the joint committee on cannabis
5 policy, who shall serve as co-chairs of the commission; 1 member appointed by the speaker of
6 the house of representatives; 1 member appointed by the president of the senate;1 member
7 appointed by the minority leader of the house of representatives; 1 member appointed by the
8 minority leader of the senate; the executive director of the cannabis control commission or their
9 designee; the attorney general or their designee; and the treasurer or their designee.
1 of 2

10 SECTION 3. The commission shall submit its report, along with any recommendations,
11 to the clerk’s office of the house of representatives and the senate no later than 1 year after the
12 passage of this act.
