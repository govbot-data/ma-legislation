An Act authorizing the grant of easements in or takings of certain parcels of land to the city of
Boston.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. The City of Boston, acting by and through its Parks and Recreation
