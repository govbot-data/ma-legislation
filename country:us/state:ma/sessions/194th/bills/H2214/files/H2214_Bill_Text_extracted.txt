An Act relative to substance use disorder evaluations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 51 ½ of chapter 111 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (h) the following subsection:
3 (i) The department of public health shall annually collect, in a manner to be determined
4 by the department, the frequency and location of substance abuse evaluations ordered pursuant to
5 this section. The department of public health shall include in the report the number of persons
6 who received a substance abuse evaluation and returned to an acute care facility within the same
7 calendar year for treatment related to an opioid overdose. The department shall report such
8 information to the joint committee on health care financing, the joint committee on mental health
9 and substance abuse, and the house and senate committees on ways and means not later than
10 January 1, annually.
