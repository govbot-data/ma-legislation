HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2019 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3794
By Representatives Ultrino of Malden and Howard of Lowell, a petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3794) of Steven Ultrino and Vanna Howard relative to the fees for application for
certain drivers' licenses and state identification cards for non-drivers. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act ensuring access to state IDs for low-income people.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 8 of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended, in line 201, by inserting after the words "age of 70." the
3 following sentence:-
4 No fees associated with an application for a license shall be charged for a person at or
5 below 300 per cent of the federal poverty guidelines.
6 SECTION 2. Section 8E of said chapter is hereby amended, in line 78, by inserting after
7 the words "age of 70." the following sentence:-
8 No fees associated with an application for an identification card shall be charged for a
9 person at or below 300 per cent of the federal poverty guidelines.
