HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, June 9, 2026.
The committee on Ways and Means, to whom was referred the Bill
authorizing the town of Billerica to transfer control of certain land of the
town situated in the town of Billerica for recreational purposes for the
purposes of constructing the Yankee Doodle Bike Path (House, No. 4440),
reports recommending that the same ought to pass with an amendment
substituting therefor the accompanying bill (House, No. 5492).

AARON MICHLEWITZ.

An Act authorizing the town of Billerica to transfer control of certain land in the town of
Billerica for the Yankee Doodle Bike Path.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, pursuant to
2 section 5A of chapter 3 of the General Laws the conservation commission of the town of
3 Billerica may transfer to the select board of the town of Billerica, for recreational purposes and
4 subject to Article 97 of the Amendments of the Constitution of the commonwealth, certain land

11 and “PUE-5” on said plan of land were acquired by the town in a deed recorded in the Middlesex
