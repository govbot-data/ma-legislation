In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to create a facilities loan program for private special education schools.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the Massachusetts School
2 Building Authority, under the authority granted to it under the School Modernization and
3 Reconstruction Trust Fund pursuant to section 35BB of chapter 10 of the General Laws, shall
4 promulgate regulations for the creation of a no-interest or low-interest loan program for facility
5 construction and upgrades at schools approved under chapter 71B.
