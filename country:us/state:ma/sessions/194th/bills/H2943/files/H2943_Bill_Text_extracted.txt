By Representative Ramos of Springfield, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2943) of

The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to employment benefits of certain employees of the Department of
Developmental Services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1. Section 3 of chapter 32 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting
2 after the word “prisoners” in line 252 the following words:
3 employees of the Department of Developmental Services and Massachusetts Commission
4 for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing holding the positions of Human Services Coordinator A/B or
5 Human Services Coordinator I, Human Services Coordinator C or Human Services Coordinator
6 II, Human Services Coordinator D or Human Services Coordinator III, Residential Supervisors
7 A/B or Residential Supervisors I, Residential Supervisors C or Residential Supervisors II, and
8 Residential Supervisors D or Residential Supervisors III, as well as other DDS positions that
9 provide care, custody and supervision to individuals with developmental disabilities, who are
10 employed in such capacities for 10 years or more.
