HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, April 8, 2026.
The committee on Public Health, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 2378) of Nicholas A. Boldyga relative
to patient access to certain health care services; and the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 2462) of Patrick Joseph Kearney for
legislation to permit the scattering of unclaimed cremated remains at sea,
reports (under the provisions of House Rule 27) recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5330) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
MARJORIE C. DECKER.
