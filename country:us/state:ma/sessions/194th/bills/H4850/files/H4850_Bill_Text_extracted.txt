HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, December 18, 2025.
The committee on Revenue, to whom was referred the message from
Her Excellency the Governor recommending legislation empowering
municipalities and local governments (House, No. 56), reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 4850) ought to be
adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.

3 the Governor’s Message empowering municipalities and local governments (House, No. 56).
4 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
5 and its recommendations, if any, together with drafts of legislation necessary to carry such
6 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
7 on or before January 6, 2026.
