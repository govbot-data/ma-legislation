68 immediate need shall be sufficient to establish eligibility for immediate housing placement.
69 (b) On the same business day in which any family requests emergency housing
70 assistance, the executive office shall inquire whether the family is in immediate need of
71 emergency housing assistance. If the family is determined to be in immediate need of emergency
72 housing assistance, the executive office shall offer the family shelter placement to begin that
73 same business day, unless the executive office possesses specific information confirming that the
