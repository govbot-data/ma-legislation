An Act relative to the department of unemployment assistance.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 23 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following
2 section:-
3 Section 28. (a) The executive office of labor and workforce development shall convene a
4 special team to conduct a review of the department of unemployment assistance. The special
5 team shall consist of 5 members, to be appointed by the secretary of labor and workforce
6 development, who shall have expertise in administrative operations and technology and
7 unemployment insurance. The special team shall review the department of unemployment
8 assistance in the following categories: (i) technology and resources; (ii) fraud; (iii) equitable
9 access; and (iv) timeliness. Following its review of the department of unemployment assistance,
10 the special team shall draft an action plan to reform and modernize the department.
11 (b) Not later than July 1, the special team shall submit to the secretary of labor and
12 workforce development, the clerks of the house of representatives and the senate and the joint
1 of 2

13 committee on labor and workforce development a report including: (i) a summary of the special
14 team’s review; (ii) the special teams action plan, including any recommendations for legislative
15 or regulatory changes; and (iii) an estimate of the cost for making the changes recommended in
16 the action plan.
17 SECTION 2. Chapter 29 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
18 section 2JJJJJJ the following section:-
19 Section 2KKKKKK. (a) There shall be established and set up on the books of the
20 commonwealth a separate fund to be known as the Unemployment Assistance Review Fund, to
21 be administered by the secretary of labor and workforce development. There shall be credited to
22 the fund: (i) any appropriations or other money authorized by the general court and specifically
23 designated to be credited to the fund; (ii) funds from public and private sources, including gifts,
24 grants and donations; and (iii) interest earned on any money in the fund. The secretary may make
25 expenditures from the fund without further appropriation for the purposes of implementing any
26 changes recommended by action plan of the special team established pursuant to section 28 of
27 chapter 23.
28 (b) Annually, not later than July 1, the secretary shall submit to the clerks of the house of
29 representatives and the senate, the house and senate committees on ways and means and the joint
30 committee on labor and workforce development a report on the activities of the fund.
