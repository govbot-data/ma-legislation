Dear Mr. Hurley:
Pursuant to Chapter 47 of the Acts of 2017, Section 57, which amended GL. Chapter 123 §35,
the Commissioner of Mental Health is required to report annually whether a facility other than
the Massachusetts Correctional Institution at Bridgewater, Massachusetts Alcohol and Substance
Abuse Center (MASAC) is being used for the treatment of males committed to MASAC under
Section 35, and the number of persons so committed to such a facility in the previous year.
Although the Department of Mental Health does not maintain this information, I have inquired of
the Department of Correction, and have received the following information:
For calendar year 2024, the Department of Correction reports that 556 men were admitted to
MASAC at Plymouth. Of this number, 370 men were admitted under “straight” Section 35
orders, 61 were under Section 35 and had cash bail orders in connection with criminal matters,
and 125 were under Section 35 and were either held without bail or held due to Violation of
Probation.
Please do not hesitate to contact the Department of Correction if you need additional
information.
Sincerely,
Beth Luas, MPA, OT
Acting Commissioner
cc: Shawn Jenkins, Commissioner, Department of Corrections
