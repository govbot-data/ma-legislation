HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 30, 2026.
The committee on Aging and Independence, to whom were referred
the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 755) of Brian M. Ashe and
Kelly W. Pease relative to social card games at municipal senior centers;
the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 757) of Tackey Chan
relative to mandated reporting of elder abuse; the petition (accompanied
by bill, House, No. 759) of Rob Consalvo relative to monitoring devices in
resident rooms at certain long-term care facilities; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 761) of Manny Cruz relative to the

relative to acute hospital and nursing home governance; the petition
