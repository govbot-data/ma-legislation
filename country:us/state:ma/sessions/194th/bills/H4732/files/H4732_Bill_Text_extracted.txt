The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 17, 2025.
The committee on Municipalities and Regional Government, to whom
were referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2245) of Bruce
J. Ayers relative to commercial breeder dog kennels; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 2256) of Mark J. Cusack relative to the
care and sale of cats, dogs and rabbits; and the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 2333) of Margaret R. Scarsdale and others for legislation
to establish a municipal building assistance program and building authority,
reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 4732)
ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
JACK PATRICK LEWIS.

HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4732
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, November 17, 2025.
1 Ordered, That the committee on Municipalities and Regional Government be authorized
2 to sit during a recess of the General Court to make an investigation and study of House
3 documents numbered 2245, 2256 and 2333, relative to dog kennels, sales of animals and
4 municipal building.
5 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
6 and its recommendations, if any, together with drafts of legislation necessary to carry such
7 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
8 on or before December 31, 2026.
