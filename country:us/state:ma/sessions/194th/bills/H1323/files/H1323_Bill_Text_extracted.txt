43 inserting in place thereof the following definition:-
44 “Eligible dependent”, the spouse or child of an eligible person, subject to the applicable
45 terms of the health benefit place covering such employee. The child of an eligible individual or
46 eligible employee shall be considered an eligible dependent until the end of the child’s twenty-
