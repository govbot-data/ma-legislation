By Representative Montaño of Boston, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 272) of

assistance for maternal health. Children, Families and Persons with Disabilities.

(2025-2026)

An Act to protect maternal health.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the department of transitional
2 assistance shall provide financial assistance to an otherwise eligible pregnant person with no
3 dependent child beginning when the pregnancy is verified, provided that such assistance shall be
4 the amount for one person under the transitional aid to families with dependent children
5 program.
