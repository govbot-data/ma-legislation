HOUSE . . . . . . . . No. 5242
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 16, 2026.
The committee on Agriculture and Fisheries, to whom were referred
the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 115) of Antonio F. D.
Cabral for legislation to establish a division of marine fisheries resources
within the Department of Fish and Game of the Executive Office of
Energy and Environmental Affairs; the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 131) of Patrick Joseph Kearney for legislation to authorize
commercial lobster fisherman to fish according to their permit for 12
months a year in state waters; the petition (accompanied by bill, House,
No. 133) of Rita A. Mendes and Bridget Plouffe relative to dairy farm
participation in the clean peak standard incentive program; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 134) of Norman J. Orrall relative
operating off-highway or recreational vehicles that damage or destroy
certain field crop products or agricultural property; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 135) of Steven J. Ouellette relative to
the impact of proposed rules and regulations on commercial agricultural
operations; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 137) of
Lindsay N. Sabadosa and others relative to the definition of agriculture,
reports (under the provisions of House Rule 27) recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5242) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
AARON L. SAUNDERS.
