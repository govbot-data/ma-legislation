An Act authorizing the town of Billerica to ban second generation anticoagulant rodenticides
within the town.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding Chapter 132B of the General Laws or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, the town of Billerica may by By-Law prohibit or restrict the
3 application of second-generation anticoagulant rodenticides within the town of Billerica,
4 including the application of such pesticides by licensed commercial applicators as defined in 333
5 C.M.R. 10.00.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
