HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 12, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3089) of Tricia Farley-Bouvier and
Thomas W. Moakley relative to taxation of digital advertising services; and
the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3207) of Francisco E. Paulino
for legislation to modify the revenue requirements for smoking bars, reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5205) ought to be
adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
