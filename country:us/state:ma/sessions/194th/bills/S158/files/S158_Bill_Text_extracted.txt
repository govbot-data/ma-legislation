An Act increasing accessibility in restaurants for physically handicapped persons.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 13A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting, after the eighth
2 paragraph, the following:-
3 Any restaurants, including but not limited to, cafeterias, lounges, bars and other places
4 open to the public where food or beverages are served are required to have at least 5% of the
5 chairs be armchairs or have them available on-site upon request.
