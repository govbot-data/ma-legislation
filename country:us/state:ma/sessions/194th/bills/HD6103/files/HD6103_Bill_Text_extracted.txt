An Act authorizing the division of capital asset management and maintenance to lease the
LoConte Memorial Rink to the city of Medford.
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
forthwith authorize the lease the parcel of land with the LoConte Memorial Rink thereon to the
city of Medford, therefore it is hereby declared to be an emergency law, necessary for the
immediate preservation of the public convenience.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding sections 32 to 37, inclusive, of chapter 7C of the General
2 Laws, section 52 of chapter 206 of the acts of 2024 or any other general or special law to the
3 contrary, the division of capital asset management and maintenance, in consultation with the
4 department of conversation and recreation, may lease to the city of Medford for a term of up to
5 30 years, including any extensions, the parcel of land described in section 2 and the ice skating
6 rink thereon to the city of Medford for recreational purposes.
1 of 2

7 SECTION 2. The parcel of land with the LoConte Memorial Rink to be leased pursuant
8 to section 1 is located at 97 Locust Street in the city of Medford and contains 1.44 acres, more or
9 less.
10 SECTION 3. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the lease shall:
11 (i) be on such terms and conditions as the commissioner of the division of capital asset
12 management and maintenance, in consultation with the department of conservation and
13 recreation, deems advisable; and (ii) provide for the city of Medford to operate, manage,
14 maintain and repair the property, which costs shall be taken into account as part of the
15 consideration of the lease.
16 SECTION 4. If the property ceases to be used for said purposes, except for period of
17 repair or rehabilitation, the commissioner of the division of capital asset management and
18 maintenance shall terminate the lease and the property shall revert to the commonwealth under
19 the care and control of the department of conservation and recreation.
20 SECTION 5. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the city of
21 Medford shall be responsible for half of the costs and expenses of the lease of the parcel of land
22 described in section 2 as determined by the commissioner of capital asset management and
23 maintenance, which may include, but not limited to, the costs of any engineering, surveys and
24 recording fees related to the lease of said parcel to the city of Medford.
