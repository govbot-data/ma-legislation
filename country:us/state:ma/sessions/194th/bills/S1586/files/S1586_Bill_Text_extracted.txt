An Act relative to smoking cessation agents.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 94C of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting after section 19F the following section:-
3 Section 19G. (a) Notwithstanding any other law, a licensed pharmacist may dispense
4 Smoking Cessation Agents in accordance with written, standardized procedures or protocols
5 developed by an actively practicing physician registered with the commissioner to distribute or
6 dispense a controlled substance in the course of professional practice pursuant to section 7 if
7 such procedures or protocols are filed at the pharmacist’s place of practice and with the board of
8 registration in pharmacy before implementation.
9 (b) Before dispensing Smoking Cessation Agents authorized under this section, a
10 pharmacist shall complete a training program approved by the commissioner, which shall include
1 of 2

11 but not be limited to proper documentation, quality assurance and referral to additional services,
12 including appropriate recommendation that the patient follow-up with a medical practitioner.
13 (c) The department of public health, board of registration in medicine and board of
14 registration in pharmacy shall adopt regulations to implement this section.
