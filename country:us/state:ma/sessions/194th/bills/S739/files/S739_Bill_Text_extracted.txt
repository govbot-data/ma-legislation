An Act promoting insurance rate review transparency.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 117C of chapter 175 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following subsection:-
3 (16) Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the commissioner shall
4 (i) provide summaries of rate changes offered in plain language to consumers, (ii) determine a
5 time period in which advanced notice is given to consumers prior to proposed rate changes, and
6 (iii) provide consumers with official comment periods to review and comment on proposed rate
7 changes.
