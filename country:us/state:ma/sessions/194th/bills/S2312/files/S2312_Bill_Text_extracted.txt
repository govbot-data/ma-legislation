An Act relative to air monitoring stations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 111 of the General Laws as so appearing, is hereby amended by adding the
2 following new section:-
3 Section 142P. There shall be at least one Air Monitoring Station within a one-mile radius
4 of any working natural gas compressor station to collect data and verify compliance with the
5 National Ambient Air Quality Standards. Construction and maintenance of Air Monitoring
6 Stations shall be funded through the building permit paid for by the operating energy corporation
7 to the state Department of Environmental Protection. Personnel shall be staffed through the state
8 Department of Environmental Protection to collect data on a weekly basis, varying between
9 morning and evening collection times.
