SENATE DOCKET, NO. 1718 FILED ON: 1/16/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1409
By Ms. Kennedy (by request), a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1409) of Gary
Lowell, for legislation to mandate psychiatric evaluations in a hospital emergency room if a
restraint authorization is given. Mental Health, Substance Use and Recovery.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to reform mental health supports and increase public safety.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Part I, Title XVII, Chapter 123, Section 12, Subsection A of the Massachusetts General
2 Laws is hereby amended by adding the following words immediately after “such purposes by the
3 department” but before “If an examination is not possible”: “Psychiatric evaluation in a hospital
4 emergency room is mandatory when a restraint or authorization for restraint is given by a
5 physician, qualified psychologist, qualified advanced practice registered nurse, or licensed
6 independent clinical social worker as authorized by this section. It is from a hospital emergency
7 room that a determination must be made on whether to apply for the hospitalization of such
8 person for a 3-day period at a public facility or a private facility authorized for such purpose by
9 the department.”
