The Children’s Medical Security Plan Legislative Report
FY 2025– 2nd QUARTER
Reporting Period October 1, 2024– December 31, 2024
The Children’s Medical Security Plan provides access to primary and preventive health care
services to certain children and adolescents. Children from birth to age 18 are eligible for CMSP
provided they are not eligible for other MassHealth benefits (except MassHealth Limited) and
provided that they are not otherwise insured.
Effective January 1, 2025, enrollee premiums were set at zero for those enrollees whose income
is up to 300% of the Federal Poverty Level (FPL), and premiums are set via a sliding scale for
enrollees with incomes greater than 300% of the FPL. In addition, all copayments were
eliminated for CMSP members effective January 1, 2025.

Total Expenditures
The number of claims filed for CMSP for the second quarter of Fiscal Year FY 2025 totaled
185,805. There were 113,867 medical claims, 61,503 dental claims, and 10,435 pharmacy claims
filed during the second quarter of the fiscal year. Total expenditures for the second quarter of the
Fiscal Year 2025 were $11,729,120.
