Re: Annual Report on Interceptions of Communications for Calendar Year 2020
Dear Sirs:
Pursuant to G .L. c. 272, § 99R, I hereby submit this report to the General Court on behalf of the
Berkshire District Attorney's Office for the 2020 calendar year.
1. Number of Applications for Warrants
The total number of applications made to justices of the Superior Court for warrants pursuant to
G.L. c. 272, § 99, authorizing the interception of oral, wire, data and electronic communications
during the 2020 calendar year was: 5
2. Name of Applicant
All applications were submitted under the name of District Attorney Andrea Harrington, as well
as through her designated Assistant District Attorneys.
3. Number of Warrants Issued
The total number of warrants issued pursuant to these applications during the 2020 calendar year
was: 5

2020 Wire Report Page 2 of2
4. Effective Period for Warrants
The effective period for the warrants issued was from September 15, 2020, to November 2, 2020.
5. Number and Designation of Offenses
The warrants were sought to discover evidence of "possession or sale of a narcotic or harmful
drug" as defined in G .L. c. 272, § 99(B)(7), including trafficking fentanyl and/or heroin in violation
of G.L. c. 94C, § 32E(c ), trafficking cocaine and/or methamphetamine in violation of G .L. c. 94C,
§ 32E(b ), the distribution and/or possession with the intent to distribute such controlled substances
in violation of G.L. c. 94C, §§ 32, 32A, and the conspiracy to violate the Controlled Substances
Act in violation of G.L. c. 94C, § 40.
6. Number of Renewals:
The total number of renewals for all warrants was: 3
7. Number of Interceptions:
The total number of pertinent interceptions made during the 2020 calendar year was: 7,468
8. Number of Indictments:
The total number of indictments believed to be obtained because of these interceptions was:
38 indictments were returned against 7 individual defendants
9. Number of Criminal Convictions:
To date, seven (7) defendants have pleaded guilty to seventeen (17) charges arising from the
wiretap investigation described above, to charges including Conspiracy to Traffic Cocaine (o ver
200 grams), Trafficking Cocaine, Possession of Heroin/Fentanyl with Intent to Distribute,
Possession of Cocaine with Intent to Distribute, and Illegal Possession of a Firearm. No defendant
has yet elected to proceed to trial. The only case that remains pending in the Berkshire Superior
Court is one where the defendant has plead guilty but is currently in warrant status after failing to
appear on the scheduled day of execution of sentence.
If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
Very truly yours,
