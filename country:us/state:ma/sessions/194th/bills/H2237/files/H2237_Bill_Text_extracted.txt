An Act regarding a minor's consent to substance use treatment.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 112, Section 12E of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020 official edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking out the first sentence and replacing it thereof with the following
3 sentence:-
4 A minor twelve years of age or older who is found to suffer from a substance use disorder
5 by a medical professional may give their consent to the furnishing of hospital and medical care
6 related to the diagnosis or treatment of such substance use disorder. Such consent shall not be
7 subject to disaffirmance because of minority.
