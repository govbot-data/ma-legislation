61 SECTION 4. There shall be an independent appraisal, or appraisals, of the easements
62 described in Section 3 to be conveyed as authorized by this act to determine the full and fair
63 market value, or the value in use as proposed, based upon one or more professional appraisals as
64 commissioned by the commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance. In order to
65 expedite the conveyances authorized by this act, the commissioner may, in consultation with the
66 Department of Fish and Game, accept any appraisal or appraisals of these easements which may
67 have been conducted before the effective date of this act and which may have been accepted and
68 agreed to by the department. Algonquin Gas Transmission, LLC shall compensate the
69 Commonwealth in an amount greater than, or equal to, the full and fair market value, or the value
70 in use of these easements as proposed, whichever is greater, as determined by these independent
71 appraisals. Algonquin Gas Transmission, LLC shall assume all costs associated with any
72 engineering, surveys, appraisals, deed preparation and other expenses deemed necessary by the
73 commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance to execute the conveyances
74 authorized by this act. All money paid to the Commonwealth by Algonquin Gas Transmission,
75 LLC as a result of the conveyance of the easements authorized by this act shall be deposited in
76 the general fund of the Commonwealth.
77 The commissioner of capital asset management and maintenance shall submit these
78 appraisals and a report thereon to the inspector general for his review and comment. The
79 inspector general shall review and approve the appraisal or appraisals, and the review shall
80 include an examination of the methodology used for the appraisal or appraisals. The inspector
