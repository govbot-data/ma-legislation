An Act relative to the recovery of emergency assistance funds used on non residents with a
financial sponsor.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 30 of Chapter 23B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after subsection (G) the following subsection:-
3 (H) The executive office of housing and livable communities shall implement a policy to
4 identify any families or individuals in the emergency housing assistance program, including
5 those residing in overflow shelters, that have a financial sponsor under federal immigration law.
6 For any families or individuals identified as has having a financial sponsor, the executive
7 office shall, in consultation with the office of the attorney general, seek reimbursement of any
8 funds that should have been covered by the financial sponsor through legal action against the
9 financial sponsor.
