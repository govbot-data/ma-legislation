An Act relative to option (D) beneficiaries.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 12 of chapter 32 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by striking out the paragraph, in lines 154 to 160, inclusive, and inserting in
3 place thereof the following paragraph: -
4 Option (D), Member Survivor Allowance. — At any time, a member, upon his written
5 notice
6 on a prescribed form filed with the board prior to his or her death, may nominate one or
7 more eligible beneficiaries as set forth under option (c) of this section, who if such member dies
8 before being retired, shall receive a portion, as specified on said form, of the yearly amount of
9 the option (c)allowance to which such member would have been entitled had his retirement taken
10 place on the date of his death; provided that, in the event that multiple beneficiaries are
1 of 2

11 nominated, the benefit will be calculated using the youngest beneficiary’s age at the time of the
12 member’s death; provided further, that upon the death of a beneficiary, said beneficiary's portion
13 shall be divided equally among surviving beneficiaries.
