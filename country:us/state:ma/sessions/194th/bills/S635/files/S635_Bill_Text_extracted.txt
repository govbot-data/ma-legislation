In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to deer open hunting season.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 5 of Chapter 131 of the General Laws as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition is
2 hereby amended by adding at the end the following language:-
3 The time period for deer open hunting season by means of a primitive firearm as defined
4 under this chapter or under 321 CMR 3.00(4)(b)4 shall extend from beginning the third Monday
5 after Thanksgiving and ending on January 1 of the following year, all days inclusive.
