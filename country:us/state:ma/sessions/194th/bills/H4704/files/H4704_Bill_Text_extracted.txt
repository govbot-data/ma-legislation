HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 5, 2025.
The committee on Ways and Means, to whom was referred the Senate
Bill amending certain laws relative to individuals with disabilities (Senate
2563), reports recommending that the same ought to pass with amendments
striking out all after the enacting clause and inserting in place thereof the
text contained in House document numbered 4704; and by striking out the
title an inserting in place thereof the following title: “An Act relative to
individuals with intellectual or developmental disabilities”.
For the committee,
AARON MICHLEWITZ.
