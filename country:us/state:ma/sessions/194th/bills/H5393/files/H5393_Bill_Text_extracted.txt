HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, May 11, 2026.
The committee on Public Service, to whom was referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3936) of Danillo A. Sena for legislation
to establish a sick leave bank for Richard Curley, an employee of the
Middlesex Sheriff’s Office, reports recommending that the accompanying
order (House, No. 5393) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
DANIEL J. RYAN.
