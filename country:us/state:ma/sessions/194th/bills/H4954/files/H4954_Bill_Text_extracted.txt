An Act to increase enrollment in affordable health plan networks.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Subsection (a) of section 11 of chapter 176J of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting at the end thereof the
3 following:-
4 The commissioner may also exempt carriers from complying with this section based on
5 (i) whether the majority of a carrier’s chapter 176J membership is enrolled in a single rate
6 region; or (ii) whether the majority of the carrier’s chapter 176J membership is enrolled in
7 subsidized coverage offered under chapter 176Q through which such members receive state or
8 federal subsidies; or (iii) whether the carrier receives 80 per cent or more of its income from
9 government programs, including subsidized coverage offered under chapter 176Q through which
10 such members receive state or federal subsidies.
11 SECTION 2. Section 9A of chapter 176O, as so appearing, is hereby amended by striking
12 subsection (a) and replacing it with the following:-
