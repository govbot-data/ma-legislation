HOUSE . . . . . . . . No. 5164
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 2, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3114) of Kenneth I. Gordon relative to
senior property tax deferrals; the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
3120) of James K. Hawkins and Steven S. Howitt for legislation to provide
municipalities with the option to freeze residential tax rate or valuation for
the elderly with means tested criteria; the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3235) of Adam J. Scanlon and Simon Cataldo relative to tax
relief for low-income seniors and veterans; the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 3241) of Adam J. Scanlon relative to establishing a senior
property tax deferral pilot program; the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 3272) of Tommy Vitolo and David M. Rogers relative to senior
property tax deferral; the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3968)
of William F. MacGregor relative to property tax exemptions for seniors
and persons with disabilities; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House,
No. 4027) of Bruce J. Ayers relative to motor vehicle excise tax exemptions
for persons with disabilities, reports recommending that the accompanying
order (House, No. 5164) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
