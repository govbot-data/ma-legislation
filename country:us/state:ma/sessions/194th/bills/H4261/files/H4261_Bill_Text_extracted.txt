An Act regulating kratom sales in the Commonwealth (Ty's Bill).
Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its purpose, which is to
prevent deaths with the unregulated sale of Kratom, therefore it is hereby declared to be an
emergency law, necessary for the immediate preservation of the public health.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:

2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after CLASS A, subsection (d)(8) in line 103,

4 (9) 7-hydroxymitragynine
