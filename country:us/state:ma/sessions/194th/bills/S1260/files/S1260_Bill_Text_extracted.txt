_______________
An Act relative to violent or sexual offenses committed by those using the Emergency
Assistance shelter system.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 30 of Chapter 23B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting at the end thereof the following paragraph:-
3 "Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, no individual convicted or
4 charged with a violent felony or a sexual offense pursuant to chapter 265 of the General Laws, as
5 so appearing, while receiving benefits pursuant to this chapter shall be eligible to continue to
6 receive such benefits absent a written waiver issued by the director of the Massachusetts
7 emergency assistance program."
