An Act to enhance child passenger safety.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 7AA of Chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing the 2016 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by inserting after the first paragraph, the following paragraph:-
3 The child passenger restraint shall be approved by the US Department of Transportation’s
4 Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards 213 and must be properly fastened and secured
5 according to the manufacturer’s instructions. A child who is younger than two years of age must
6 be restrained in a rear-facing child passenger restraint system with an internal harness, until the
7 child reaches the manufacturer’s height or weight limit of the child passenger restraint system. A
8 child who is at least two years of age must be restrained in either a rear-facing child passenger
9 restraint system with an internal harness, or a forward-facing child passenger restraint system
