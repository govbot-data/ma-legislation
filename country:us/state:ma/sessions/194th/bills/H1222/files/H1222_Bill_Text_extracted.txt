An Act relative to prescription medication re-authorization.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, any licensed health insurer
2 subject to the provisions of chapters 32A, 118E, 175, 176A, 176B or 176G of the General Laws
3 shall not request or require annual re-authorizations for any prescription drug as defined in
4 section 1 of chapter 94C of the General Laws that has been duly prescribed by a physician,
5 physician’s assistant or nurse practitioner to treat any chronic disease including, but not limited
6 to, diabetes, heart disease, Alzheimer's disease, cancer, stroke and respiratory disease
