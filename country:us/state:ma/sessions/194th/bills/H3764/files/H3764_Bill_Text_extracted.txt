An Act allowing blood types to be printed on licenses to operate motor vehicles.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 8 of Chapter 90 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following
2 paragraph:-
3 An applicant for a license or renewal thereof may provide the registrar with a statement
4 of such person’s blood type to be printed upon a REAL-ID-compliant license or a Massachusetts
5 license. The registrar shall make provision for an applicant or licensee who so chooses to list
6 such person’s blood type on the license.
