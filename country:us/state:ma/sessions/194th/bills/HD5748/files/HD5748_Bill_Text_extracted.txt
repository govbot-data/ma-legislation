By Representative Gómez of Easthampton, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Homar Gómez
that the Registry of Motor Vehicles be authorized to issue special motor vehicle license plates to
individuals who are autistic or neurodivergent or to parents or legal guardians of children who
are autistic or neurodivergent. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the issuance of special license plates for autistic and neurodivergent
individuals and their families.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1.
2 The Registry of Motor Vehicles shall establish a program for the issuance of special
3 motor vehicle license plates to individuals who are autistic or neurodivergent, or to parents or
4 legal guardians of children who are autistic or neurodivergent.
5 SECTION 2.
6 Eligibility for such special license plates shall be limited to owners or lessees of private
7 passenger motor vehicles registered in the Commonwealth. An application for such a plate must
8 include a certification from a licensed physician, psychologist, or other qualified medical
9 professional, attesting that the applicant, or their child, has been diagnosed as autistic or
10 neurodivergent.
1 of 2

11 SECTION 3.
12 The design of the special license plate shall include distinctive features or markings
13 identifying the vehicle as associated with an autistic or neurodivergent individual or their family.
14 The Registry shall consult with representatives of the autistic and neurodivergent community in
15 the development of the plate’s design. The plate shall be of the same size and general shape as
16 standard license plates.
17 SECTION 4.
18 The fee for the issuance of a special license plate under this act shall be equal to the
19 standard registration fee for passenger vehicles. The license plate shall be valid for a biennial
20 period and may be renewed upon payment of the applicable renewal fee.
21 SECTION 5.
22 The Registrar may promulgate rules and regulations necessary for the implementation of
23 this act, including but not limited to procedures for application, verification of eligibility, and
24 renewal.
25 SECTION 6.
26 This act shall take effect upon passage.
