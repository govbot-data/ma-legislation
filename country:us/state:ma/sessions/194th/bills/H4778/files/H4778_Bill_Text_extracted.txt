HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, December 1, 2025.
The committee on Community Development and Small Businesses, to
whom were referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 301) of
Daniel J. Hunt relative to industrial development finance authorities; the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 310) of Chynah Tyler for
legislation to establish within the Department of Housing and Community
Development a commission to review equitable city planning and
development; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 311) of
Chynah Tyler and Joan Meschino relative to the calculation of area median
income in municipalities with a population over 100,000 residents, reports
the accompanying order (House, No. 4778) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANDRES X. VARGAS.
