_______________

_______________
An Act updating the unit pricing exemption threshold.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 115A of chapter 6 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following sentence:- For the purposes of this section, the
3 calculation of a retail establishment’s sales volume shall exclude gross receipts from the sale of:
4 (i) lottery tickets or shares sold pursuant to chapter 10; (ii) prepaid gift cards or stored value
5 cards, regardless of where any such card may be redeemed, which are sold to consumers at face
6 value or a premium or discount thereto; and (iii) motor fuels.
