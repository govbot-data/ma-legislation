An Act to amend the general officer designation in the state staff of the militia.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 33 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting, in Section 15(a), in line
2 3, by adding after the word “general.” the following:
3 “The adjutant general shall be the commanding general of all military forces, organized
4 militia units, and the military division of the Commonwealth.”
5 And by striking, in Section 15(b), the following: “provided, however, that the adjutant
6 general shall not personally exercise command of troops.”
