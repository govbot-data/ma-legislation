HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, July 13, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 5160) of Adam J. Scanlon for an
investigation by a special commission (including members of the General
Court) relative to the distribution of lottery revenues to municipalities; and
the joint petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 5260) of Lindsay N.
Sabadosa and Joanne M. Comerford relative to taxation of certain foreign
government pension benefits, reports recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5575) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
