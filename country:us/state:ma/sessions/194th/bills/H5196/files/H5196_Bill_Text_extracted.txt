The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 9, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3023) of Bruce J. Ayers for legislation to
exempt small charities from the tax on raffle proceeds; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3028) of David Biele for legislation to
further regulate the room occupancy excise; the petition (accompanied by
bill, House, No. 3038) of Antonio F. D. Cabral and others for legislation to
establish a downtown vitality fund to strengthen local business districts and
main streets funded by a portion of the sales tax; the petition (accompanied
by bill, House, No. 3118) of Richard M. Haggerty for legislation to establish
a special commission (including members of the General Court) to conduct
a comprehensive study relative to generating revenue from digital
advertising; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3263) of
Erika Uyterhoeven for legislation to establish a tax on local revenues from
digital advertising, reports recommending that the accompanying order
(House, No. 5196) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
