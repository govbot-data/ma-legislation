of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 28 of chapter 32A, as so appearing in the 2024 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by adding, after clause (vi), the following clause:-
3 “(vii) prescription medications related to the provision of gender affirming health care
