In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to melt ICE defenses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 233 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following new section:-
3 Section 84. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, a federal agency
4 memorandum shall not constitute a defense to a civil violation of state law, including but not
5 limited to tort actions.
6 SECTION 2. Chapter 265 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition,
7 is hereby amended by adding the following new section:-
8 Section 61. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, a federal agency
9 memorandum shall not constitute a defense to a violation of state criminal law, including but not
10 limited to violations of this chapter.
