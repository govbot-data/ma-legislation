HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5057 FILED ON: 8/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4691
By Representatives Hawkins of Attleboro and Pease of Westfield, a petition (subject to Joint
Rule 12) of James K. Hawkins and Kelly W. Pease relative to establishing an Orange Star license
plate for veterans exposed to Agent Orange. Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act establishing an Orange Star license plate for veterans exposed to Agent Orange.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The registrar shall furnish, without charge to owners of private passenger motor vehicles
2 who reside in Massachusetts, the Orange Star plate. Upon evidence deemed satisfactory by the
3 registrar, registration plates bearing the Orange Star distinctive emblem that identifies Vietnam
4 veterans who have a documented medical condition that the VA attributes to service in Vietnam
5 for one private passenger motor vehicle or motorcycle owned and principally used by such
6 recipient; provided, however, that the surviving spouse of a deceased recipient may elect to
7 retain such distinctive registration plates or emblem for personal use upon payment of the
8 established registration fee for private passenger motor vehicles or motorcycles and an additional
9 annual twenty dollar fee until such spouse remarries or fails to renew or cancels such
10 registration. This recognition is not officially a military medal, but a symbol of appreciation for
11 those affected by Agent Orange.
