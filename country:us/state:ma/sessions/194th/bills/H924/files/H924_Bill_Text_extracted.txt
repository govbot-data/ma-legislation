By Representative Davis of Great Barrington, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 924)
of Leigh Davis for legislation to authorize the Great Barrington Fire District to supply the
inhabitants of the town of Great Barrington with water for domestic and other purposes.
Environment and Natural Resources.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to the Great Barrington Fire District.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 335 of the acts of 1905 is hereby amended by striking out section 4
2 and inserting in place thereof the following section:-
3 Section 4. The Great Barrington Fire District may supply any of the inhabitants of the
4 town of Great Barrington with water for domestic and other purposes, upon such terms as the fire
5 district may determine.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
