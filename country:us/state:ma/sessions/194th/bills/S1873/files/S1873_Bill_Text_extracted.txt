An Act relative to the collective bargaining rights for employees of the committee for public
counsel services.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 1 of chapter 150E of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding to the end of the seventh paragraph, titled
3 "Employer" or "public employer", the following sentence:- In the case of employees of the
4 committee for public counsel services, employer shall mean the committee for public counsel
5 services or its designee.
6 SECTION 2. Section 7 of said chapter 150E of the General Laws, as so appearing, is
7 hereby amended by striking the word "or" before the word “commission” and inserting after the
8 word “commission” in line 15 the following words:- or the committee for public counsel
9 Services.
1 of 2

10 SECTION 3. Said section 7 of said chapter 150E of the General Laws, as so appearing, is
11 hereby amended by striking the word "and" before the words “the state lottery commission” and
12 inserting after the word “commission” in line 32 the following words:- and committee for public
13 counsel services.
