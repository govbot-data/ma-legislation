_______________
1 Ordered, That a special joint committee on initiative petitions be established for the
2 purpose of considering the initiative petitions transmitted to the Clerk of the House of
3 Representatives by the Secretary of the Commonwealth in accordance with the requirements of
4 Article XLVIII of the Amendments to the Constitution; and be it further
5 Ordered, That the special joint committee shall consist of 10 members as follows: 5
6 members of the senate, 4 of whom shall be appointed by the president of the senate and 1 of
7 whom shall be appointed by the minority leader of the senate; and 5 members of the house of
8 representatives, 4 of whom shall be appointed by the speaker of the house and 1 of whom shall
9 be appointed by the minority leader of the house.
