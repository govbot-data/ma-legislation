11 SECTION 5. Section 5 is hereby further amended after line 53 by inserting the following
12 paragraph:-
13 One representative of the Gateway Municipality rider community population shall serve
14 on the advisory board as a voting member for a term of 3 years. Each Gateway Municipality in
15 the region, as defined by section 3A of chapter 23A, shall appoint a representative that is an
16 active rider of the regional transit authority; provided, however, that the representative of the
17 disabled community, rider community and gateway municipality rider community shall not be
18 appointed by the same city or town in the region. For authority regions that contain more than
19 one Gateway Municipality, each municipality shall appoint a representative on a rotating basis in
20 an order as determined by the board. The mayor or city manager and the chairman, town
21 manager or town administrator shall appoint a resident of the city or town for this purpose.
