An Act relative to updating and modernizing Chapter 40R.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 9 of chapter 40R of the General Laws, as so appearing, is
2 hereby amended by striking out subsections (a) and (b) and inserting in place thereof the
3 following 2 subsections:-
4 (a) The commonwealth shall pay from the trust fund or other funds from appropriations
5 or other money authorized by the general court a zoning incentive payment, according to the
6 following schedule:
7 Projected Units of New Construction% Payment
8 Up to 20% $20,000
9 21 to 100% $150,000
10 101 to 200% $400,000

11 201 to 500% $740,000
12 501 or more% $1,200,000
13
14 Subject to any conditions imposed by the department as a condition of approving a smart
15 growth zoning district or starter home zoning district, the zoning incentive payment shall be
16 payable upon confirmation of approval of the district by the department. The projected number
17 of units shall be based upon the zoning adopted in the smart growth zoning district or starter
18 home zoning district.
19 (b) The commonwealth shall pay from the trust fund or other funds from appropriations
20 or other money authorized by the general court a 1-time density bonus payment to each city or
21 town with an approved smart growth zoning district and a 1-time production bonus payment to
22 each city or town with an approved starter home zoning district. This payment shall be $6,000
23 for each housing unit of new construction created in the smart growth zoning district and $6,000
24 for each housing unit of new construction created in the starter home zoning district. The amount
25 due shall be paid on a unit-by-unit basis in accordance with department regulations, upon
26 submission by a city or town of proof of issuance of a building permit for a particular housing
27 unit or units within the district.”
28 SECTION 2. Section 12 of Chapter 40R of the General Laws is hereby amended by
29 inserting in line 3, after the word “review”, the following words:-
30 “, including but not limited to an analysis of the incentive payments, in Section 9, to
31 determine that those payments are consistent with housing costs per the Consumer Price Index.”
2 of 3

32 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect for tax years beginning on or after January 1,
33 2025.
34 SECTION 4. Notwithstanding any other section of the law to the contrary, no later than
35 10 years after the date of enactment, the increased incentives, as identified in section 1 and
36 section 2, shall revert back to their original figures.
