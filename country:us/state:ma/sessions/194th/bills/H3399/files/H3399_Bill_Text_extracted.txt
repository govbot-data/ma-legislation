An Act to promote Massachusetts manufacturers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 30B of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting at the end thereof the following new section:-
3 Section 24. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary and to the extent
4 permitted by federal law, a state agency or governmental body shall procure a minimum of ten
5 percent of the contract value of products by businesses, as defined in section 3A of chapter 23A
6 of the general laws, provided, that the commonwealth is their principal place of business, and
7 provided further, that a waiver process is set forth by the awarding authority.
