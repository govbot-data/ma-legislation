HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 687 FILED ON: 1/10/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 1388
By Representative Marsi of Dudley, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1388) of John J.
Marsi that the maximum temporary absence for nonmedical leave paid for by MassHealth be
increased from ten days to fourteen days. Health Care Financing.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act extend nursing home resident overnights with their family from 10 to 14.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The division of medical assistance, under authority granted in section 12 of chapter 118E
2 of Massachusetts general laws, shall amend the Massachusetts long term care regulations (130
3 CMR 456.431). Such amendment shall increase the maximum temporary absence for
4 nonmedical leave paid for by MassHealth, from ten days to fourteen days.
