HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 2084 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2801
By Representative Decker of Cambridge, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2801) of
Marjorie C. Decker relative to health insurance benefits for certain surviving spouses. Public
Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to health insurance for surviving spouses.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 11 of chapter 32A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out in lines 6 and 7 the words "until the
3 remarriage of said surviving spouse".
4 SECTION 2. Section 9B of chapter 32B of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
5 amended by striking out in lines 4 and 5, the words ",until the remarriage or death of said
6 surviving spouse".
7 SECTION 3. Section 9B of chapter 32B of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby
8 amended by striking out in lines 9, 10 and 11, the words “the entire cost of such premium
9 without any contribution by the governmental unit” and inserting in place thereof the following
10 words: - one-half the cost of such premiums with one-half the cost of such premiums paid by the
11 governmental unit.
12 SECTION 4. Section 9D of said chapter 32B is hereby repealed.
1 of 2

13 SECTION 5. The provisions in sections 1 and 2 of this act shall apply prospectively to
14 surviving spouses of a deceased employee or retired employee, who remarried prior to the
15 effective date of this act and whose insurance coverage, upon remarriage, was terminated under
16 chapters 32A or 32B of the General Laws.
17 SECTION 6. The provisions in sections 1, 2 and 3 of this act shall be prospective from
18 the effective date of this act and shall not entitle any surviving spouse of a deceased employee or
19 retired employee to any retroactive insurance coverage.
