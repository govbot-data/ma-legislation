An Act strengthening GPS tampering Laws.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 209A is hereby further amended in section 7 by inserting after the word “system”
2 at the end of the eighth paragraph the following new paragraph:-
3 “The intentional removal or destroying of said global positioning satellite tracking device
4 with the goal of evading authorities or to cause harm without judicial approval or without a
5 showing of necessity to prevent greater harm may constitute a felony."
