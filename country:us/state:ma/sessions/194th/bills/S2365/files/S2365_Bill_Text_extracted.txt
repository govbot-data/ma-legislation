An Act relative to human trafficking awareness.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6C of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after Section 31 the
2 following section:-
3 Section 31A. (a) The office of planning and programming shall provide a sample sign, to
4 be approved by the general manager. Such sign shall be not less than eight and one-half inches
5 high and eleven inches wide containing the following notice:-
6 “WARNING. Obtaining forced labor or services is a crime under Massachusetts law. If
7 you or someone you know is being forced to engage in any activity and cannot leave – whether it
8 is commercial sex, housework, farm work or any other activity – call the National Human
9 Trafficking Hotline: 1-888-373-7888. You can remain anonymous, and the Hotline is available
10 24 hours a day, seven days a week.”
1 of 2

11 Such notice shall be provided in English, Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese, and Portuguese,
12 and any other language as recommended by the office of planning and programming, whereby
13 the English notice must cover at least one-half of the sign, to display in all state highway and
14 mass transit facilities and vehicles that are open to the public, including restrooms, in a
15 conspicuous manner clearly visible to the public and employees.
16 (b) The office of planning and programming shall periodically, and at least annually,
17 consult with the attorney general as to whether a sample sign pursuant to subsection (a) shall be
18 provided in any other language.
