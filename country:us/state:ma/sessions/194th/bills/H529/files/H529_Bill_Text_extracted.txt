In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to reduce waste in school cafeterias.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 CHAPTER 111, SECTION 223 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 SUBSECTION (g), the following- “(h) The Massachusetts School Building Authority shall
3 promulgate regulations requiring applicants for funding for construction of new schools or
4 substantial renovations of school kitchens (1) to install dishwashers which will allow for the use
5 of reusable foodware, (2) to adopt procedures and an enforcement plan for reducing solid waste,
6 including recyclable materials and food scraps.”
