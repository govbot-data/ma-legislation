An Act authorizing the Plymouth County Retirement System to grant creditable service to Judy
Hartigan.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law, rule or regulation to the
2 contrary, the Plymouth county retirement system shall credit Judy Hartigan, the Rockland Town
3 Collector, with creditable service for the service she rendered for the Town of Rockland for the
4 period of April 11, 2009 to the present, inclusive, for the purpose of determining her
5 superannuation retirement allowance pursuant to paragraph (a) of subdivision (2) of section 5 of
6 chapter 32 of the General Laws. Before the date that any retirement allowance becomes effective
7 in the case of Judy Hartigan, she shall pay into the annuity savings fund of the retirement system
8 in one sum or installments, upon such terms and conditions as the retirement system may
9 prescribe, an amount equal to that which would have been withheld as regular deductions from
1 of 2

10 her regular compensation for such previous service, or most recent portion thereof, as she may
11 elect, had she been a member of the Plymouth county retirement system during the period the
12 service was rendered, plus regular interest thereon.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
