Senate, May 5, 2025 -- (Filed by Mr. Rodrigues) Order relative to procedures to be followed
when considering the House Bill making appropriations for the fiscal year 2026 for the
maintenance of the departments, boards, commissions, institutions, and certain activities of the
Commonwealth, for interest, sinking fund, and serial bond requirements, and for certain
permanent improvements.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
Senate, May 05, 2025.
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
