An Act protecting inmate safety and the expenditure of state funds.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 127 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by adding the following section:-
3 Section 172. No inmate or prisoner placed in the custody of a sheriff shall be transported
4 outside of the commonwealth for the purposes of labor or public or community service
5 programming unless there exists a bona fide public health or safety emergency. If such a bona
6 fide public health or safety emergency exists, the sheriff shall file a description of the purpose,
7 the proposed mode of transportation, the estimated duration of the time to be spent outside of the
8 commonwealth, the housing and security arrangements to be made during the proposed stay and
9 the estimated cost of the entire undertaking with the secretary of administration and finance, the
10 secretary of the executive office of public safety and security, the chairs of the joint committee
1 of 2

11 on public safety and homeland security and the chairs of the senate and house committees on
12 ways and means and shall obtain written approval from the secretary of administration and
13 finance and the secretary of the executive office of public safety and security prior to the
14 commencement of said transport.
