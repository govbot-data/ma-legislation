An Act authorizing the town of Boxborough to prohibit the use or sale of second-generation anti-
coagulant rodenticides.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding chapter 132B of the General Laws or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, the town of Boxborough may regulate through local bylaw or board
3 of health regulation the use or sale of second generation anti-coagulant rodenticides within the
4 town of Boxborough, including prohibiting the use of such pesticides by licensed commercial
5 applicators as defined in 333C.M.R.10.00.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
