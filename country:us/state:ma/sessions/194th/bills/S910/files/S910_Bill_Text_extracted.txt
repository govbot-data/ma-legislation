An Act relative to association health plans.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary and to the maximum extent
2 allowed by federal law a Multiple Employer Welfare Arrangement or Association Health Plan
3 shall be allowed to operate in the Commonwealth, provided that it meets the following
4 requirements:
5 1. The arrangement shall at all times be in compliance with federal law and regulation,
6 including but not limited to the US Department of Labor’s regulations regarding Association
7 Health Plans.
8 2. The arrangement’s governing documents shall require the arrangement to be
9 actuarially sound, and the arrangement shall in fact be actuarially sound.
1 of 2

10 3. The arrangement shall be conducted in accordance with Massachusetts General Laws
11 Chapter 266 section 30 and Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 93A.
2 of 2
