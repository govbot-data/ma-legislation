By Representative Kearney of Scituate, a petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4317) of
Patrick Joseph Kearney and Steven George Xiarhos that the State Retirement Board be
authorized to grant creditable service for certain employment with the federal fire department at
the Otis Air National Guard Base. Public Service.
