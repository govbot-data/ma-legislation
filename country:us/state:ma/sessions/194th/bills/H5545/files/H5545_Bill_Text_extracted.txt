An Act establishing an amendment to Bridgewater Town Charter: Article IV, Section 4-1 Town
Manager term of office.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 ORDERED that pursuant to M.G.L., Chapter 89 Section 8 of the Massachusetts
2 Constitution, that allows the legislative body of the Town of Bridgewater to vote to submit a
3 Special Act to the State Legislature to Amend the current Bridgewater Town Charter; the Town
4 Council voted to amend said Town Charter Article IV Section 4-1 as follows:
5 Add the following language:
6 The Town Council shall appoint by a majority vote of the full Town Council, a Town
7 Manager for a term not to exceed three (3) years, which may be renewed for additional terms of
8 up to three (3) years each upon a majority vote of the full Town Council, subject to the
9 procedures for appointment and removal outlined in this Article.
1 of 2

10 Said Amendment shall only become effective and it contingent upon approval by the
11 Town Council, the State Legislature and the voters of the Town of Bridgewater.
