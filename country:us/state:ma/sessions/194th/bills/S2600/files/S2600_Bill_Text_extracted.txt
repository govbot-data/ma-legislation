Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:

3 “Scalp cooling system”, any device used to cool the human scalp to prevent or reduce

13 SECTION 3. Chapter 175 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
14 section 47UU, the following:-
15 Section 47VV. Coverage for scalp cooling systems
16 Section 47VV. (a) For the purpose of this section the following terms shall have the
17 following meanings:-
18 “Scalp cooling system”, any device used to cool the human scalp to prevent or reduce
19 hair loss during cancer chemotherapy treatment, provided that such device is designed and
20 intended for repeated use and is primarily and customarily used to serve a medical purpose
21 (b) Every policy delivered or issued for delivery in the commonwealth that provides
22 medical, major medical, or similar comprehensive type explanation coverage and provides
23 coverage for cancer chemotherapy treatment shall provide coverage for scalp cooling systems
24 used in connection with cancer chemotherapy treatment. Coverage provided under this section
25 may be subject to annual deductibles and coinsurance, including copayments consistent with
26 those established for other benefits within a given policy. Scalp cooling system coverage
27 pursuant to this section shall not exceed an amount of $300 per year.
