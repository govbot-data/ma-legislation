HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 3412
By Representatives Sabadosa of Northampton and Montaño of Boston, a petition (accompanied
by bill, House, No. 3412) of Lindsay N. Sabadosa and Samantha Montaño for the issuance of an
annual proclamation by the Governor to designate September 20th as LGBTQ+ veterans day.
State Administration and Regulatory Oversight.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to LGBTQ+ Veterans Day.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section
2 15CCCCCCC the following section:-
3 Section 15DDDDDDD. The governor shall annually issue a proclamation setting apart
4 September 20th as LGBTQ+ Veterans Day, to celebrate lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and
5 queer (LGBTQ+) veterans who have honorably served in the U.S. Armed Forces throughout the
6 nation’s history and recommending that the day be observed in an appropriate manner by the
7 people.
