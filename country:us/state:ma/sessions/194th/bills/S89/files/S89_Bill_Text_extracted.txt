SENATE DOCKET, NO. 2380 FILED ON: 1/17/2025
SENATE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 89
By Ms. Miranda, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 89) of Liz Miranda for legislation
to allow the delivery of cannabis by licensed delivery operators or couriers to any municipality in
the commonwealth. Cannabis Policy.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act expanding access to legal, regulated cannabis delivery.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Whereas, The deferred operation of this act would tend to defeat its
2 purpose, which is to immediately remove unnecessary burdens from vulnerable social equity
3 businesses before the end of the license exclusivity period, therefore it is hereby declared to be
4 an emergency law, necessary for the immediate preservation of the public convenience.
5 SECTION 2. Section 4 Chapter 94G of the General Laws is amended by inserting, at the
6 end of the section, the following subsection:-
7 (m) The commission shall not prohibit the delivery of cannabis by licensed delivery
8 operators or couriers to any municipality in the commonwealth nor to any establishment that
9 provides overnight lodging services to tourists and visitors, including but not limited to hotels,
10 motels, and resorts.
