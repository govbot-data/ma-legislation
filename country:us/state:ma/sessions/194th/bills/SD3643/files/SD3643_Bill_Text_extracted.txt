An Act to uncover ICE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 2 of chapter 90 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024 Official Edition, is
2 hereby amended by inserting after the seventh paragraph the following paragraph:-
3 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the registrar shall not issue
4 confidential or undercover registration plates to United States immigration and customs
5 enforcement or its sworn officers or agents for any stated justification, including, but not limited
6 to, for the purposes of undercover law enforcement. Any such previously issued confidential
7 registration shall be revoked not later than 30 days after the effective date of this paragraph and
8 shall not be eligible for renewal.
