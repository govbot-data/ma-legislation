In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act ensuring transparency in research funding.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 42B of Chapter 63 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 subsection (e) the following subsection:-
3 (f) A corporation under this section shall be required to:
4 (1) disclose the amount, type, and source of all current and pending research support
5 received by, or expected to be received by, the corporation for all qualified exemption years;
6 (2) certify that the disclosure is current, accurate, and complete; and
7 (3) agree to update such disclosure at request at any time determined appropriate.
