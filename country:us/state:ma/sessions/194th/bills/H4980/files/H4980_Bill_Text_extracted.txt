180 SECTION 5. Chapter 93B of the General Laws, as so appearing, is hereby amended by
181 inserting after section 9, the following new section:-
182 Section 9A. (a) As used in this section, the following words shall have the following
183 meanings unless the context clearly requires otherwise:
184 “Adverse determination”, a determination by the manufacturer or distributor that the
185 amount to be paid to the dealer is less than what the dealer requested in its warranty or recall
186 claim for reimbursement to said manufacturer or distributor pursuant to section 9.
187 ''Independent mediator'', a person not a party to a dispute who enters into a written
188 agreement with the parties to assist them in resolving their disputes and has completed at least
189 thirty hours of training in mediation and who either has four years of professional experience as a
190 mediator or is accountable to a dispute resolution organization which has been in existence for at
191 least three years, and has some background and experience with motor vehicle warranty claims
192 submitted by dealers to a manufacturer or distributor.
9 of 12

193 (b) Neither a dealer nor a manufacturer or distributor may bring an action in a court of
194 competent jurisdiction based on an alleged violation of the following sections unless the dispute
195 is submitted to mediation pursuant to this section prior to filing an action in court: (i) disputes
196 concerning the proposed average percentage markups on parts under clause (i) of paragraph (2)
197 of subsection (b) of section 9; (ii) disputes concerning the proposed average labor rates under
198 clause (ii) of said paragraph (2) of said subsection (b) of said section 9; (iii) disputes concerning
199 time allowances for the diagnosis or performance of warranty work under paragraph (3) of said
200 subsection (b) of said section 9; and (iv) disputes concerning warranty service or part recall
201 service claims charged back to the dealer under subsection (d) of said section 9.
202 Prior to filing an action in a court of competent jurisdiction, the dealer, manufacturer or
203 distributor shall serve a demand for mediation not later than sixty days after the dealer’s receipt
204 of the manufacturer’s or distributor’s adverse determination on the matter being submitted to
205 mediation. A demand for mediation must be in writing and served on the dealer, manufacturer or
206 distributor by certified mail, through a nationally recognized overnight delivery service, or
207 through any method permitted under the parties’ franchise agreement at an address designated
208 within the franchise agreement. The demand for mediation must contain a brief statement of the
209 dispute and the relief sought. The recipient of such demand shall be obligated to diligently
210 participate in good faith in such mediation process. Within twenty-one days after the date a
211 demand for mediation is served, the parties shall cooperate in good faith to select an independent
212 mediator for the purpose of attempting to resolve the dispute. In the absence of an agreement
213 within said twenty-one days, the attorney general shall appoint an appropriate independent
214 mediator within ten days of the request by either party. Mediation shall be conducted and
215 completed within sixty days of service of the demand for mediation, unless otherwise extended
10 of 12

216 by agreement of the parties, and the costs will be shared equally between the dealer, on one hand,
217 and the manufacturer or distributor on the other hand.
218 Notwithstanding any provision in this section to the contrary, the service of a demand for
219 mediation shall stay the time period for filing any action under section 9, until thirty days after
220 the mediation has been completed. Mediation shall be deemed completed when the mediator has
221 so indicated in writing, which may include a writing sent via electronic mail.
222 To the extent this section conflicts with any dispute resolution process set forth in a
223 franchise agreement or other agreement between dealer and manufacturer or distributor, this
