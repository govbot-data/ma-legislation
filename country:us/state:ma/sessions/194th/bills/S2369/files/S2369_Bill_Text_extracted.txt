An Act relative to partial payment of fines relating to driver’s license suspension or revocation.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 33 of chapter 90 of the General Laws is hereby amended by striking out the
2 thirty-seventh paragraph and inserting in place thereof the following paragraphs:-
3 (37) The registrar shall permit any person owing fees related to suspensions or
4 revocations of any license or right to operate a motor vehicle to enter into a monthly payment
5 plan with the registrar to pay any fees related to the reinstatement of said license or right to
6 operate a motor vehicle; provided, however, that the minimum monthly payment due shall be
7 $25.00. The registrar may accept automatic monthly payments by credit or debit card on file with
8 the registrar or payments made by check or money order received by mail or in person. Upon
9 receipt of the first payment due on a monthly payment plan established pursuant to this
10 paragraph the registrar may issue a conditional reinstatement of said license or right to operate a
1 of 2

11 motor vehicle if the person is otherwise qualified to be granted said license or right to operate a
12 motor vehicle. The registrar shall revoke a conditional reinstatement of a license or right to
13 operate a motor vehicle if a required monthly payment is more than 30 days in arrears or for
14 violation of any motor vehicle law listed in chapter 90 of the general laws. Upon payment in full
15 of the fees owed, the registrar shall fully reinstate the license or right to operate a motor vehicle
16 if the person is otherwise qualified to be granted said license or right to operate a motor vehicle.
17 (38) For the registration of every street rod, replica vehicle, specially constructed vehicle
18 or custom vehicle, as defined in section 2H consistent with the vehicle's intended use and the
19 requirements of 540 CMR 2.05.
