HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 9, 2026.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom were referred so much of the recommendations of the Inspector
General (House, No. 11) as relates to creating the Office of the Inspector
General Recovery Fund (House, No. 17), and so much of the
recommendations of the Department of the State Treasurer (House, No. 35)
as relates to treasury operations (House, No. 38), reports recommending that
the accompanying order (House, No. 5198) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.

The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, March 9, 2026.
1 Ordered, That the committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight be
2 authorized to sit during a recess of the General Court to make an investigation and study of
3 House documents numbered 17 and 38, relative to creating the Office of the Inspector General
4 Recovery Fund and treasury operations.

7 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
8 on or before December 31, 2026.
