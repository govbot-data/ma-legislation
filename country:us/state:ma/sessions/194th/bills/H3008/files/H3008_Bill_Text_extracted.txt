An Act relative to non-contiguous farm land.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 4 of Chapter 61A of the general laws is hereby amended by adding at the end of
2 the first paragraph thereof, the following new sentences:
3 Whenever an owner holds two or more non-contiguous areas of land in one or more
4 subdivisions of the commonwealth equaling not less than five acres, said owner shall have right
5 to apply for the provisions of this section provided all parcels are within a 10-mile radius of one
6 another, or within the confines of a single municipality. Said acreage shall meet all the
7 requirements for the benefits of this section as if the land was contiguous.
