Marcus S. Vaughn relative to business relocation after receiving economic development
incentive program grant funding. Economic Development and Emerging Technologies.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to business relocation after receiving state grant funding.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 3D of chapter 23A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting the following subsection:-
3 (f) All EDIP contracts, as defined by section 3A of this chapter, awarded by the EACC to
4 a corporation, sole proprietorship, limited liability company, or partnership for a certified project,
5 as defined by said section 3A, shall include the requirement that, unless it declares bankruptcy as
6 defined by U.S.C. 11, a corporation, sole proprietorship, limited liability company, or partnership
7 shall forfeit or refund any credits or grants received pursuant to an EDIP contract if the
8 corporation, sole proprietorship, limited liability company, or partnership relocates outside the
9 commonwealth within 20 years. The office of the attorney general shall promulgate regulations
10 pursuant to the enforcement of this subsection.
