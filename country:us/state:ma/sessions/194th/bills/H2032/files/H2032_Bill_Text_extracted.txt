An Act relative to grandparent visitation rights.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The second paragraph of section 39D of chapter 119 of the General Laws, as appearing
2 in the 2022 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following 2 sentences:- The
3 petition shall include a verified complaint that the allegations therein are made on personal
4 knowledge, set forth facts that would be admissible in evidence, affirmatively show the affiant's
5 competence to testify on the matters therein, and are not conclusory. Upon the filing of the
6 petition, the court shall, at the sole expense of the petitioning grandparent and without input from
7 any party, appoint a guardian ad litem for the minor child pursuant to section 56A of chapter 215
8 and assign the issue of grandparent visitation for mediation.
