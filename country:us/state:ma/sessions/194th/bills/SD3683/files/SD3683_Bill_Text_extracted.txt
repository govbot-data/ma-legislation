RE: Settlements and Judgments Report —Fiscal Year 2026 Quarter 2
Dear Chair Michlewitz and Chair Rodrigues:
Pursuant to Section 2 of Chapter 9 of the Acts of 2025, the Office of the Comptroller hereby
submits to the House and Senate Committees on Ways and Means a report on payments from the
Settlements and Judgments reserve (1599-3384) for the second quarter of Fiscal Year 2026.
The Settlements and Judgements reserve entered Fiscal Year 2026 with a $1,000,000
appropriation in the GAA and $4,977,479 from prior appropriation continued, for a total of
$5,977,479 in the reserve. The Fiscal Year 2026 GAA also provided the ability to spend in
anticipation based on the average of the prior five years spending. In July 2025, the spend in
anticipation ceiling was set at $20,306,986 based on the historical average. The table below
shows the five-year history:
Fiscal Year S&J Expenditure
FY 2021 $11,808,677.07
FY 2022 $12,446,561.43
FY 2023 $27,843,361.47
FY 2024 $29,917,459.94
FY 2025 $19,518,870.22
5-Year Average $20,306,986.03

Amounts paid under the spend in anticipation authorization will require a supplemental
appropriation in the current fiscal year. As shown above, a $9,976,057 million appropriation
would be required to address the current deficit. However, that deficit should be expected to
grow as the remaining spend in anticipation capacity is used up. When that occurs, my office
will not be able to make any payments without a substantial additional appropriation.
As shown in the five-year table, the total amount of expenditure varies by year. Given the trends
so far in Fiscal Year 2026, it appears likely that the total spending for the year could be in the
$27 million - $30 million range. We began Fiscal Year 2026 with $5,977,479 in appropriated
funds including the prior appropriation continued. I request consideration of supplemental
funding of $25 million to carry us through to the fiscal year end. Any amounts remaining at year
end could be continued into Fiscal Year 2027.
A detailed report is attached that provides information on payees, amounts, and the associated
department or agency for settlements and judgments paid during Quarter 2 from appropriation
account 1599-3384. This information is also posted quarterly on the Comptroller’s transparency
website, CTHRU, which may be accessed via this link, https://www.macomptroller.org/cthru.
