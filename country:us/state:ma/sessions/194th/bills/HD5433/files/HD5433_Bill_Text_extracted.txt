SUBJECT: November 2025 UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE TRUST FUND
INFORMATION
The following information relates to the reporting on the Unemployment Insurance
Trust Fund (“UI Trust Fund”) for the month of October 2025.
As of October 31, 2025, the preliminary Massachusetts UI Trust Fund ending balance
was $1.90 billion.
Year-to-date through October 31, 2025, preliminary employer contributions totaled $1.44
billion.
Year-to-date through October 31, 2025, DUA paid $1.96 billion in benefits for regular UI
for 690,802 initial and continuing claims.
This update contains information related only to actual dollars collected or disbursed. On
January 17, 2025, the Commonwealth and the U.S. Department of Labor entered into an
agreement that fully resolved any outstanding liability related to pandemic
unemployment insurance overdraws as a result of federal UI programs established
during the COVID-19 pandemic. The impact of this agreement is reflected in the October
2025 projections contained in the Unemployment Insurance Trust Fund Annual Outlook

Report. The Commonwealth’s first payment under the agreement is due December 1,
2025.
The UI Trust Fund data is also available at Mass.gov/UITrustFund in the form of data
visualizations spanning from October 2024 through October 2025.
