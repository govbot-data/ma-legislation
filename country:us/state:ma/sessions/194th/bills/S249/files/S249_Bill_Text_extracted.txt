An Act relative to the cancellation of gym memberships.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 93 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting at the end of section 81
2 the following:-
3 "Any seller whose website allows for the purchase of a contract for health club services
4 or the adjustment of a contract, such as but not limited to, additional services, programs, and
5 benefits or the removal of additional services and benefits, shall have available on their website a
6 method of canceling contracts online."
