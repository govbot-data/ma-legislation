An Act relative to the oversight of cable contracts.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 166A of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 10 the
2 following section:-
3 Section 10A. If an issuing authority determines that a licensee has violated a condition of
4 their license, the matter shall be referred to the department of telecommunications and cable for
5 further review and action pursuant to section 11. If a settlement agreement is reached between
6 the issuing authority and a licensee, the department shall review and approve the agreement;
7 provided, that the department shall not approve the agreement if the department determines that
8 the agreement is not in the best interest of impacted subscribers.
