An Act relative to a town-wide ban on the use of second-generation anticoagulant rodenticides
(SGARs).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding Chapter 132B of the General Laws or any other general or special law to
2 the contrary, the Town of Bedford may by ordinance prohibit or restrict the application of
3 second-generation anticoagulant rodenticides within the Town of Bedford, including the
4 application of such pesticides by licensed commercial applicators as defined in 333 C.M.R.
5 10.00, except as allowed by the Board of Health to remediate a public health condition.
