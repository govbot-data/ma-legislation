63 by striking out the first paragraph and inserting in place thereof the following paragraph:-

65 renewal period, satisfactorily complete courses or programs of instruction approved by the
66 board; provided, however, that attendance at such courses or programs of instruction shall be not
67 less than 6 hours but not more than 12 hours as determined by the board. The curriculum
68 contained in such courses or programs shall contain: (i) not less than 2 hours on fair housing law;
69 and (ii) not less than 4 hours selected from any of the following subjects: equal employment
70 opportunity, accessibility for individuals with disabilities, agency law, environmental issues in
71 real estate, zoning and building codes, real estate appraisal and financing, property tax
72 assessments and valuation and real estate board regulations.
