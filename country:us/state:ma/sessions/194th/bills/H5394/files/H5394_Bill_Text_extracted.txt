HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, May 14, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom was recommitted so much of the
message from Her Excellency the Governor recommending legislation
relative to empowering municipalities and local governments [for message,
see House, No. 56] as relates to sections 45 to 72, inclusive, 85 and 86,
reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5394)
ought to be adopted [Senators Eldridge, Jehlen, Brady, Keenan and Rausch
dissent].
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.

HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5394
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, May 14, 2026.
1 Ordered, That the committee on Revenue be authorized to sit during a recess of the
2 General Court to make an investigation and study of sections 45 to 72, inclusive, 85 and 86 of
3 the Governor’s Message empowering municipalities and local governments (House, No. 56).
4 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
5 and its recommendations, if any, together with drafts of legislation necessary to carry such
6 recommendations into effect, by filing the same with the Clerk of the House of Representatives
7 on or before December 31, 2026.
