HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 1909 FILED ON: 1/15/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 2221
By Representatives Lewis of Framingham and Higgins of Leominster, a petition (accompanied
by bill, House, No. 2221) of Jack Patrick Lewis, Natalie M. Higgins and others for legislation to
require that student identification cards contain suicide prevention lifeline contact information.
Mental Health, Substance Use and Recovery.
[SIMILAR MATTER FILED IN PREVIOUS SESSION
SEE HOUSE, NO. 1999 OF 2023-2024.]
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to student mental health.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Chapter 71 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after
2 section 37R the following section:-
3 Section 37S. A public school, including a charter school, that serves students in any of
4 grades 6 to 12, inclusive, and that issues new or replacement student identification cards to
5 enrolled students shall include on such cards the telephone and text message number for the 988
6 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
7 SECTION 2. Chapter 73 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following
8 section:-
1 of 2

9 Section 21. A public or private institution of higher education that issues new or
10 replacement student identification cards to enrolled students shall include on such student
11 identification cards the telephone and text message number for the 988 Suicide and Crisis
12 Lifeline.
13 SECTION 3. As of the effective date of this act, if a public school subject to section 37S
14 of chapter 71 of the General Laws or a public or private institution of higher education subject
15 section 21 of chapter 73 of the General Laws has a supply of unissued student identification
16 cards that do not comply with the requirements of said section 37S of said chapter 71 or said
17 section 21 of said chapter 73, the school may issue such student identification cards to students
18 until such remaining supply is depleted.
19 SECTION 4. Section 37S of chapter 71 of the General Laws and section 21 of chapter 73
20 of the General Laws shall apply to student identification cards issued on or after July 1, 2025.
