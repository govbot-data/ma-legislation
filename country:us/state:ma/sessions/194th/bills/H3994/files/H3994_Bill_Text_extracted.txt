An Act relative to insurance settlements.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Paragraph (1) of section 48 of chapter 152 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by inserting after the first sentence the following 3
3 sentences:- The employer shall be notified in writing of such payment by the insurer by way of a
4 lump sum agreement and shall have 30 days to consent in writing or object in writing to the lump
5 sum agreement. After the passage of 30 days, if the employer does not object or consent in
6 writing to the proposed lump sum agreement, then the employer’s consent is not required. In this
7 event the insurer shall file an affidavit, to be attached to the lump sum agreement papers as an
8 exhibit that states: (i) the employer has been notified of the proposed lump sum agreement; (ii)
9 no written objection has been received by the employer; and (iii) 30 days has passed from the
10 date of mailing of the notice to the employer.
