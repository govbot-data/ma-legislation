legislation relative to AI-generated child sexual abuse material. Advanced Information
Technology, the Internet and Cybersecurity.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to AI-generated child sexual abuse material.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 29C of chapter 272 of the General Laws is hereby amended by
2 inserting after the words "visual reproduction" the following words:- ", including visual material
3 produced by digitization".
4 SECTION 2. Section 31 of said chapter of the General Laws is hereby amended by
5 inserting before the definition of “dissemination” the following definition:-
6 “Digitization”, the creation or alteration of visual material including, but not limited to,
7 through the use of computer-generated images, in a manner that would appear to a reasonable
8 person to depict a minor.
9 SECTION 3. Said section of said chapter is hereby further amended by inserting, at the
10 end of the definition of "visual material", the following sentence:- The term "visual material"
11 shall include visual material produced by digitization.
