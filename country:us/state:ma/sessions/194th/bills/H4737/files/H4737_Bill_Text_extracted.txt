HOUSE DOCKET, NO. 5288 FILED ON: 10/23/2025
HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4737
By Representative Puppolo of Springfield, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Angelo J.
Puppolo, Jr., and others for the issuance of an annual proclamation by the Governor to designate
May 2 as veterinary youth day. State Administration and Regulatory Oversight.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to veterinary youth day.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 6 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after Section
2 15EEEEEEE, added by chapter 32 of the acts of 2025, the following section:-
3 Section 15FFFFFFF. The governor shall annually issue a proclamation setting apart May
4 2 as Veterinary Youth Day to call attention to the youth of the commonwealth to get involved in
5 veterinary medicine, to raise awareness of the shortage of veterinarians in commonwealth and
6 the importance of animal welfare and to call for schools, individuals and communities to partner
7 with local veterinarians and animal shelters in the commonwealth to expose students to careers in
8 veterinary medicine and shall recommend that the day be observed in an appropriate manner by
9 the people.
