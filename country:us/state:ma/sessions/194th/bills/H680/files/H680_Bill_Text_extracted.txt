An Act relative to mental health counselors in schools.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 71 of the General Laws, as amended by section 219 of chapter 238 of the acts of
2 2024, is here by further amended by adding the following section:
3 Section 102. (a) For the purpose of this section, the following terms shall, unless the
4 context clearly requires otherwise, have the following meanings:
5 “School mental health counselor”, a person licensed or eligible for licensure as a mental
6 health counselor under section 165 of chapter 112.
7 “School psychologist”, a person licensed or eligible for licensure as a licensed
8 educational psychologist under section 165 of chapter 112 who has been certified as a school
9 psychologist by the department of education.
10 (b) Subject to appropriation, each public school shall employ school mental health
11 counselors or school psychologist that have been granted by the commissioner a provisional or
12 standard certificate of employment, pursuant to section 38G, for grades 3 to 12. During the
1 of 2

13 school year, a school mental health counselor or school psychologist shall have at least 1 meeting
14 with each student to ensure that the student: (i) is aware the counselor or psychologist is
15 available to them; (ii) becomes familiar with the counselor or psychologist; and (iii) understands
16 that the counselor or psychologist is accessible to them for whenever they may need.
