An Act relative to juror comprehension.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 62 of Chapter 234A of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official
2 Edition, is hereby amended by inserting, after the word “terminology.” in line 7, the following:-
3 The court shall provide each juror a physical information card clearly defining the
4 charges and legal terms applicable to the trial for which the juror was selected. The court shall
5 replace the card upon request.
