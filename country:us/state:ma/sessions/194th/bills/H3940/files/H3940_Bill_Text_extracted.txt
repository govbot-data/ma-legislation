_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act relative to restricting telemarketer phone numbers.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 159C of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following section:-
2 Section 15. (a) A telephone solicitor shall not make or cause to be made an unsolicited
3 telephonic sales call to a consumer using local phone numbers or area codes that are not linked to
4 their physical location or place of business in the commonwealth.
5 (b) The attorney general shall enforce and may issue fines to telephone solicitors who
6 violate this section pursuant to section 8.
7 (c) The attorney general shall adopt rules and regulations to implement this section.
8 (d) The attorney general shall report violations of this section to the advisory group
9 pursuant to section 14. The advisory group will provide mechanisms for consumers to track
10 complaints.
