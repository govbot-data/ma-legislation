_______________
An Act authorizing the city of Watertown to lease property at 124 Watertown street, Watertown
(former Parker school).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 1. Notwithstanding any provisions of chapter 30B of the general laws or of any
2 other general or special law to the contrary, the city of Watertown is hereby authorized to lease,
3 for terms of no more than thirty (30) years, space at that certain property located at 124
4 Watertown street in the city of Watertown, the former Parker school, which property is described
5 in an order of taking, recorded January 18, 2023, with the vote of its City Council, and on such
6 terms and conditions and for such consideration as the City Council deems appropriate, in
7 accordance with such process that will ensure that such leases shall be undertaken in accordance
8 with an open, fair, and competitive disposition process, using sound business practices and
9 principles of fair dealing, which process may recognize as a criteria for evaluation for such leases
10 a level of preference for current lessees of such property. Further, any design and construction to
11 be performed by a lessee or or lessees shall be exempt from the provision of the general laws
1 of 2

12 related to the construction, reconstruction, alteration, remodeling, repair and maintenance of the
13 improvements at the public property, including sections 44 to 58, inclusive of chapter 7C;
14 sections 44A to 44F, inclusive of chapter 149; and section 39M of chapter 30, provided,
15 however, the provisions of section 26 to 27H, inclusive, of chapter 149 shall apply to any
16 contracts entered into by the lessee or lessees for the construction of such improvements.
17 Section 2. This Act shall not exempt the City from the provisions of section 3 of chapter
18 40 and sections (a), (b), and (g) of section 16 of chapter 30B of the general laws, or any other
19 general or special law which requires a vote of the City Council to authorize the lease of real
20 property.
21 Section 3. This act shall take effect upon passage.
