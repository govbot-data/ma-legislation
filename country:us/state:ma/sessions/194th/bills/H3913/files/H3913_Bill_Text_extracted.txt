155 Administrator may request a public hearing by filing a written request for such a hearing with the
156 Select Board. Thereafter, a public hearing shall be held not more than thirty (30) days after the
157 request is filed, nor sooner than twenty (20) days after the filing of the request. The Town
158 Administrator may file a written statement in justification of their tenure in office with the Select
159 Board, provided any such statement is received by the Board not more than forty-eight (48)

162 amended by striking it out in its entirety and inserting in place thereof the following new
163 subsection:- The Select Board may adopt a final resolution of removal, which may be made
164 effective immediately, by affirmative vote of the majority of the full Board, at any time after ten
165 (10) days following the date of delivery of a copy of the preliminary resolution to the Town
166 Administrator, if the Town Administrator has not requested a public hearing, or at any time after
167 the public hearing if they have requested one. Within ten (10) days following the vote to make
168 the resolution final, but in no event more than forty-five (45) days following the date of adoption
169 of the preliminary resolution, the Select Board shall, by a majority vote of the full Board, either
170 affirm or deny the final resolution of removal. Failure to affirm a final resolution of removal
171 shall nullify the vote to remove the Town Administrator.
