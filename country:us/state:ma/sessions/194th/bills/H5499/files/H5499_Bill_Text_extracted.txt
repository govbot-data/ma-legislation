HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 5499
By Representative Williams of Springfield, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Bud L.
Williams and Alice Hanlon Peisch for legislation to authorize teachers employed by the
Springfield Empowerment Zone to become members of the Massachusetts Teachers' Retirement
System. Public Service.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act to include teachers from the Springfield Empowerment Zone in the Massachusetts
Teachers' Retirement System.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Subsection (i) of section 1J of chapter 69 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2024
2 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following 2 sentences:-
3 Teachers employed by the Springfield Empowerment Zone Partnership, Inc. shall be
4 members of the teachers’ retirement system pursuant to chapter 32 and a teacher’s service for the
5 Springfield Empowerment Zone Partnership, Inc. shall be creditable service pursuant to said
6 chapter 32. For the purposes of this subsection, the word “teacher” shall mean a person who
7 works on a basis of not less than half-time service in a position that would make them eligible to
8 be a member of the teachers’ retirement system if their position were performed in a public
9 school.
