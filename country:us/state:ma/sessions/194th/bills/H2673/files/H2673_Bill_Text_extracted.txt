An Act relative to loaning personal property.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Section 32 of Chapter 135 of the Acts of 2024, is hereby amended by
2 striking subsection 121B(b) in its entirety, and putting in place thereof:
3 (b) All firearm transactions within the commonwealth, including, but not limited to, all
4 purchases, sales, rentals, leases, or other transfers shall be reported to the electronic firearms
5 registration system. All firearm transactions shall be reported by all parties to the transaction via
6 the electronic firearms registration system within 7 days of the sale, rental, lease, or other
7 transfer; provided, however, that no report shall be required for a loan of a firearm to a duly
8 licensed or exempted person for a period of less than 1 year.
