HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 12, 2026.
The committee on Public Health, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 2504) of Brian W. Murray and
Christopher Hendricks relative to consumer access and the right to
practice complementary and alternative health care services; and the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 2563) of Bud L. Williams and
others for legislation to establish a dentist and dental hygienist compact,
reports recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5214)
ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
MARJORIE C. DECKER.
