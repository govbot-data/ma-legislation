HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, November 17, 2025.
The committee on Transportation, to whom was referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3694) of Steven S. Howitt for
legislation to prohibit vehicles operating on a spare tire from driving in the
passing lane, reports recommending that the accompanying order (House,
No. 4725) ought to be adopted [Representative Armini of Marblehead
dissents].
For the committee,
JAMES ARCIERO.

HOUSE . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . No. 4725
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
House of Representatives, November 17, 2025.
