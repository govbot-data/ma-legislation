In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act increasing the membership of the board of health of the town of Marblehead from three
members to five.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the number of
2 members of the board of health of the town of Marblehead shall be increased from 3 members to
3 5 members, with the select board annually electing a chairperson from among its members.
4 SECTION 2. At the first annual town election to occur following the effective date of this
5 act, 3 board of health members shall be elected. The candidate receiving the highest number of
6 votes in that election shall serve a 3-year term. The candidate receiving the second highest
7 number of votes shall serve a 2-year term. The candidate receiving the third highest number of
8 votes shall serve a 1-year term. Thereafter, as the terms of the board members expire, successors
9 shall be elected for terms of 3 years.
10 SECTION 3. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
