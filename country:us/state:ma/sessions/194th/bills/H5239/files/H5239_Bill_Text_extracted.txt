HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, March 16, 2026.
The committee on Revenue, to whom were referred the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 3153) of Hannah Kane, Patrick Joseph
Kearney and Michael O. Moore relative to the state tax return filings for
annuities; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 4128) of Paul
McMurtry for an investigation by a special commission (including
members of the General Court) relative to the threshold amounts for
lottery and gambling taxation, reports recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5239) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ADRIAN C. MADARO.
