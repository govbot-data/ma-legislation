35 Section 10AA. For the purposes of this section, the following words shall have
36 the following meanings unless the context clearly requires otherwise:
37 “Acute treatment services”, 24-hour medically supervised addiction treatment for
38 adults or adolescents provided in a medically managed or medically monitored inpatient facility,
39 as defined by the department of public health, which provides evaluation and withdrawal
40 management and that may include biopsychosocial assessment, individual and group counseling,
41 psychoeducational groups and discharge planning.
42 “Clinical stabilization services”, 24-hour clinically managed post detoxification
43 treatment for adults or adolescents, as defined by the department of public health, usually
44 following acute treatment services for substance abuse for individuals beginning to engage in
45 recovery from addiction, which may include intensive education and counseling regarding the
46 nature of addiction and its consequences, relapse prevention, outreach to families and significant
47 others and aftercare planning, for individuals beginning to engage in recovery from addiction.
48 “Transitional support services”, short-term, residential support services, as
49 defined by the department of public health, usually following clinical stabilization services,
50 which provide a safe and structured environment to support adults or adolescents through the
51 addiction recovery process and the transition to outpatient or other step-down addiction recovery
52 care.
53 The division and its contracted health insurers, health plans, health maintenance
54 organizations, behavioral health management firms and third-party administrators under contract
55 to a Medicaid managed care organization or primary care clinician plan shall cover the cost of
