By Mr. O'Connor, a petition (accompanied by bill, Senate, No. 1606) of Patrick M. O'Connor for
legislation to require AEDs in clubs and fraternal organizations and that mandatory training be
provided to all staff and members. Public Health.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act requiring AEDs in clubs and fraternal organizations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. (a) Any group organized under Chapter 180 of the General Laws shall
2 provide and maintain at least 1 fully functional automated external defibrillator, also known as
3 AED, for emergency use.
4 (b) Any group or organization that is required to provide and maintain an AED pursuant
5 to subsection (a) shall provide mandatory training in accordance with the standards established
6 by the American Heart Association or the American National Red Cross on cardiopulmonary
7 resuscitation and the use of AEDs to: all organizational staff; all members of the organization;
8 any other individuals as determined by the leadership of the organization.
9 SECTION 2. There shall be a fund, subject to appropriation, established to assist any
10 group required by subsection (a) to provide an AED with procurement, maintenance, and
11 training necessary under Section 1.
