An Act relative to summary process.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 239 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding, after section 14, the
2 following new section:-
3 Section 15. Two-tier process;
4 (a) All summary process cases shall be handled in a two-tier process. (i) First tier. This
5 event will be for the parties to determine the status of the case, explore the availability of
6 resources, attempt mediation or other opportunity to reach a resolution to the case, and identify
7 next steps in preparing the case for trial. No default or dismissal may enter if a party does not
8 appear at the first tier event. (ii) Second tier. For cases which are not resolved, the Clerk’s Office
9 shall send a written notice to the parties of the trial date.
