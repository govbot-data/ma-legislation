HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, May 7, 2026.
The committee on State Administration and Regulatory Oversight, to
whom was referred the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 3330) of
Kimberly N. Ferguson and others relative to municipal light plants, reports
(under the provisions of House Rule 27) recommending that the
accompanying order (House, No. 5372) ought to be adopted.
For the committee,
ANTONIO F. D. CABRAL.

4 Said committee shall report to the General Court the results of its investigation and study
