HOUSE . . . . . . . . No. 5104
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
________________________________________
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 17, 2026.
The committee on Higher Education, to whom were referred the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1436) of Natalie M. Higgins,
Carmine Lawrence Gentile and others for legislation to establish a grant
program to pay the equivalent of tuition and mandatory fees to eligible
students at Massachusetts public higher education institutions; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 1437) of Natalie M. Higgins and others
that institutions of higher education adopt policies on academic transcript
notations and appeals relating to sexual misconduct; the petition
(accompanied by bill, House, No. 1446) of Patrick Joseph Kearney
relative to college student immunization for meningococcal disease; the
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1460) of Alan Silvia and
Christopher Hendricks relative to residential preference for financial aid at
public institutions of higher education; the petition (accompanied by bill,
House, No. 1464) of Chynah Tyler relative to the use of the Reggie Lewis
Track and Athletic Center by members of the abutting residential
community and students, faculty, staff, and alumni at Roxbury
Community College; and the petition (accompanied by bill, House, No.
4113) of Tram T. Nguyen and Christine P. Barber relative to addressing
sexual misconduct by certain higher education employees, reports
recommending that the accompanying order (House, No. 5104) ought to
be adopted.
For the committee,
DAVID M. ROGERS.
