An Act protecting public higher education student information.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 66 of the General Laws is hereby amended by inserting after section 21 the
2 following section:-
3 Section 22. Public institutions of higher education, as set forth in section 5 of chapter
4 15A, and municipally owned institutions of higher education, shall not be required to produce
5 education records, including information designated by the public institution of higher education
6 as directory information, as defined under the Family Educational and Privacy Rights Act, 20
7 U.S.C. section 1232 (g). This section shall not apply to requests from federal, state, or municipal
8 agencies.
