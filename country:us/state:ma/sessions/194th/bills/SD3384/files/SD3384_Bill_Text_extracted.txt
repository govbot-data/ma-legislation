Re: Commission on the Status of Persons with Disabilities
Disabled Persons Protection Commission Designee
Dear Messrs. Curly and Carroll:
Pursuant to M.G.L. c. 3, §74, a designee of the Disabled Persons Protection Commission (“DPPC”)
serves on the Commission on the Status of Persons with Disabilities (“Commission”). The DPPC’s
designee on the Commission had previously been Andrew Levrault, Deputy General Counsel.
Please be advised that as of November 14, 2025, the DPPC’s designee will be Victoria R. Gill, Director
of Learning and Development.
The DPPC looks forward to its continued service on and collaboration with the Commission. Thank you
for your attention to this matter.
Sincerely,
