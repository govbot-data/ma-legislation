An Act relative to college tuition and admissions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 69 of the General Laws is hereby amended by adding the following section:-
2 Section 37. In making decisions relating to admissions, scholarships and grants, any
3 college, university, community college or junior college within the system of public institutions
4 of higher education as set forth in section 5 of chapter 15A, shall identify and give priority to
5 students or prospective students residing in the commonwealth: (1) whose biological parents are
6 deceased or had their parental rights terminated due to abuse or neglect; and (2) who have been
7 legally adopted by a blood relative within the third level of consanguinity or were assigned a
8 permanent legal guardian by a court.
