An Act to establish free access to rides to elections in Massachusetts (FARE).
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, the Massachusetts Bay
2 Transportation Authority and regional transit authorities organized under chapter 161B of the
3 General Laws shall not charge riders a fare for rapid transit, bus, and trolleybus service along
4 fixed routes on the day of a biennial state primary or biennial state general election.
