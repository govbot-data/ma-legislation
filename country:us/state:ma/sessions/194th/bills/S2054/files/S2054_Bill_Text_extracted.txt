An Act establishing a tax credit for teachers purchasing supplies.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Paragraph (a) of part B of section 3 of chapter 62 of the General Laws, as
2 appearing in the 2010 Official Edition, is hereby amended by adding the following
3 subparagraph:-
4 (16) An amount as described in section 62(a)(2)(D) of the Code for certain expenses of
5 elementary and secondary school teachers, only to the extent the amount is not otherwise
6 deducted under subparagraph (7).
7 SECTION 2. Section 1 shall be effective for tax years beginning on or after January 1,
8 2026.
