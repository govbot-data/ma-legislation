By Representative Diggs of Barnstable, a petition (subject to Joint Rule 12) of Kip A. Diggs that
the Cape Cod Regional Transit Authority be authorized to designate the Hyannis Transportation
Center in the town of Hyannis as the Thomas S. Cahir Cape Cod transportation center.
Transportation.
The Commonwealth of Massachusetts
_______________
In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act designating the Hyannis Transportation Center in the town of Hyannis as the Thomas S.
Cahir Cape Cod Transportation Center.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The Hyannis Transportation Center in the town of Hyannis, shall be designated and
2 known as the Thomas S. Cahir Cape Cod Transportation Center in honor of former State
3 Representative and former Administrator of the Cape Cod Regional Transit Authority, Thomas
4 S. Cahir. The Cape Cod Regional Transit Authority shall erect and maintain suitable signage
5 bearing that designation in compliance with the standards of the authority.
