In the One Hundred and Ninety-Fourth General Court
(2025-2026)
_______________
An Act authorizing the town of Tewksbury to lease the North Street Elementary School for a
term of years not exceeding 99 years.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding section 3 of chapter 40 of the General Laws or of any
2 other general or special law to the contrary, but subject to paragraphs (a), (b) and (g) of section
3 16 of chapter 30B of the General Laws, the town of Tewksbury may lease the North Street
4 Elementary School for a term of not more than 99 years.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
