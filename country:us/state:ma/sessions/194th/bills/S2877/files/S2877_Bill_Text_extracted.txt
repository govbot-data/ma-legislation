An Act authorizing the town of Grafton to prohibit or restrict the application of second-
generation anticoagulant rodenticides.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding chapter 132B of the General Laws or any other general or
2 special law to the contrary, the town of Grafton may by bylaw prohibit or restrict the application
3 of second generation anticoagulant rodenticides within the town of Grafton, including the
4 application of such pesticides by licensed commercial applicators as defined in 333 C.M.R.
5 10.00.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall take effect upon its passage.
