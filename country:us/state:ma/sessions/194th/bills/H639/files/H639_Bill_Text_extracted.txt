An Act relative to recognizing cheerleading as a sport.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Chapter 71 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2020 Official Edition, is hereby
2 amended by inserting after section 47 the following section:-
3 Section 47A. (a) Every school district in the commonwealth shall, directly or through an
4 authorized representative, in consultation with the department, develop guidelines, procedures
5 and safety standards for the purpose of classifying cheerleading as a sport and providing for
6 student participation in cheerleading.
7 (b) The school district or its authorized representative shall require any coach of
8 cheerleading to receive training specific to the sport of cheerleading and the safety of
9 cheerleaders.
1 of 2

10 (c) The requirements of this section shall be in addition to any requirements set forth in
11 section 47 or any other applicable provision of this chapter.
