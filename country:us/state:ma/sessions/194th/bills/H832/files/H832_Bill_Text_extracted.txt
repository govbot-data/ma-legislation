An Act relative to uniformity of ballots in polling places.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 44 of chapter 54 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:-
3 Ballots for an election shall be uniform in size, material and content for all voters,
4 regardless of whether the ballot is voted on a ballot-marking device or directly on a paper ballot;
5 provided, however, the state secretary may provide ballots that are not uniform when necessary
6 to: (i) comply with federal law; (ii) accommodate voting or tabulating; or (iii) accommodate
7 hand count tabulation methods. Ballots shall be tabulated directly from the voter-marked
8 selections and not from a barcode, QR code or other representation not marked by the voter,
9 either on a ballot-marking device or directly on paper.
