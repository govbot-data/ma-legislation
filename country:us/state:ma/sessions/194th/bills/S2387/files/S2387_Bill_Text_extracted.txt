An Act erecting Clara Barton signs in the town of Oxford.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 The Massachusetts Department of Transportation is hereby authorized and directed to
2 erect and maintain two signs on route I-395 in proximity to exit 5 northbound and southbound in
3 the town of Oxford marking and directing motorists to the birthplace of Clara Barton, founder of
4 the American Red Cross, teacher, national heroine, and native of Oxford, Massachusetts.
