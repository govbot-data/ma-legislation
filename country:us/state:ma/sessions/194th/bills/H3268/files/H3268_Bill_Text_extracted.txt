An Act providing for certain sales and use tax exemptions.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 Section 6 of chapter 64H of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022 Official Edition,
2 is hereby amended by adding the following paragraph:-
3 (yy)(1) Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, sales of non-
4 prescription breast pumps, baby lotions, baby oils, baby powders, infant supplies including baby
5 harnesses, carriages, strollers, car seats, restraints, changing tables, cribs and crib blankets,
6 diaper bags, nursing bottles, nipples, teething items or baby wipes.
7 (2)Notwithstanding any general or special law to the contrary, sales of certain apparel
8 products intended to be worn or carried on or about the human body by children 5 years of age
9 and younger, including children’s diapers, children’s clothing, children’s accessories and
1 of 2

10 children’s shoes. “Children’s clothing” shall include items intended to be worn or carried on or
11 about the human body by children 5 years of age and younger, such as shirts, pants, shorts,
12 dresses, skirts, coveralls, pajamas, socks, undergarments, suits, uniforms, outerwear, bathing
13 suits, costumes, and safety clothing. “Children’s accessories” shall include non-clothing items
14 that are intended to be worn or carried on or about the human body by children 5 years of age
15 and younger, such as gloves, hosiery, belts, bibs, hair accessories, scarves, ties and hats.
16 “Children’s accessories” shall not include items such as bags, wallets, jewelry, watches, fabrics,
17 sports equipment, umbrellas or wigs.
