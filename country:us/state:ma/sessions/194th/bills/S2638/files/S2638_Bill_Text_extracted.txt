An Act relative to retirement pension notarizations.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:
1 SECTION 1. Notwithstanding 840 CMR 15.01 or any other general or special law to the
2 contrary, no public pension system overseen by the public employee retirement administration
3 commission shall require benefit recipients to notarize any forms required for benefit
4 verification.
5 SECTION 2. Nothing in this act shall prevent the public employee retirement
6 administration, no less frequently than once every two years, from directing each retirement
7 board to require each member or beneficiary who receives a pension, retirement allowance, or
8 survivor's allowance to file with the retirement board an attestation under the penalties of
9 perjury, at such time and in such form as the board shall prescribe.
