HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES, February 9, 2026.
The committee on the Judiciary, to whom was referred the joint
petition (accompanied by bill, House, No. 1890) of Thomas W. Moakley

No. 5060) ought to pass.

MICHAEL S. DAY.
