A. Back Indexing ofRegistry of Deeds Records.
$650,000
The requested funding is to continue the procured re-indexing services for documents
recorded at the Norfolk Registry ofDeeds. The data and information on the Registry
documents recorded at the Norfolk Registry ofDeeds will be input into the document
management system utilizing current indexing standards promulgated by the Massachusetts
Registers of Deeds Association and the State Secretary's office. This project will be of
service to Registry users and those doing legal title research as well as those doing
historical and genealogical research via internet land research.
$650,000
TOTAL
All equipment and services procurement pursuant to the provisions ofMGL Ch. 30B.
